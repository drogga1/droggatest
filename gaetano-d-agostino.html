2010 11 17 Retrieve

[Fiorentina midfielder Gaetano D’Agostino is looking forward to the upcoming clash with Milan at San Siro and touched on his fitness as well as the constant comparisons to Andrea Pirlo] I’m ready. Maybe I don’t have 90 minutes in my legs yet, but the coach [Sinisa Mihajlovic] will decide. I must thank him because he is managing the situation very well. [Pirlo] has won everything, we are different. I can assure you he is one of the few players in the world who can really make a difference. Yes, he has something more than I do

[He also commented on the Italian national team and took a swipe at Cesare Prandelli’s decision to call up Lazio’s Argentine-born playmaker Cristian Ledesma] I will just say one thing: players born in a country should play for their own country. Anyway, I root for them. Give me time to get back into shape and I will compete fiercely
