Even with someone of Messi’s genius, the biggest element for a midfield trying to protect their goal is the distances between you. Instead of concentrating on the ability of one opponent, ensure you aren’t too spread out when Barcelona have the ball. That’s why I believe Liverpool should play their normal game.
