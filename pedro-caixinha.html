2018 08 03 Retrieve

[Grande? Not a grande? It’s time to put a tired Liga MX debate to bed] Tigres has a really rich squad. I’d say that Tuca has to be the reference for Mexican football managers since I’ve known it. From there to being considered a grande, from my perspective there are three points: the history a club has in a country where there are clubs with more than 100 years of existence, the trophies, we understand clearly it’s the best team in terms of football titles in the last decade, the example team in Mexico and the fans have to be national and international, not just local. A grande has to have these three things.
