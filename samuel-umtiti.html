2019 06 25 Retrieve

[Samuel Umtiti is expecting to stay at Barcelona this summer] The quality of life is great. For me, it’s the best place to play and live. The place, the mentality of the people, my team, the players that I meet daily, frankly I could not have dreamt of better. Every day I feel happy to live my passion, and what’s more to do that here in Barcelona. The adaptation went very well, I was very well received. They were very cool with me, even though I did not speak too much Spanish we managed to understand each other and everyone helped me a lot.

[He made just 13 starts in La Liga last season, but feels he is ready to kick on into 2019-20] I’m better. It’s true that it was a difficult season due to the injury but I knew it was going to happen, that there had to be a transition because I had a lot of difficulties during the World Cup. I think it was a good thing and it was worth it. At the end of the season I was able to play again and I recovered my fitness. Now I will prepare for the next season and be at 200%

2019 09 09 Retrieve

[Samuel Umtiti has rubbished reports suggesting that he had contact with Arsenal over the summer] Honestly, I don’t know where this rumour with Arsenal came from. Overnight, I received messages from my friends ‘ah, so you are joining Arsenal?’ I’m not, I’m sorry but I’m staying at Barcelona. Contacts with Arsenal? To be honest, I don’t know if they had conversations with my agent, I just told him that I didn’t care what was happening this summer and that it wasn’t even necessary to talk to me about it. It was useless, I was going to have the same answer. My dream as a child is to play for Barcelona. Last season was the most difficult of my career, especially in terms of injuries

[With Barca forced into favouring other options, Umtiti was billed as one of those who could be used as a makeweight in Barcelona’s efforts to prise Neymar away from Paris Saint-Germain] Exchange coin for Neymar? It’s crazy. It made me laugh, I don’t even have an answer to where it comes from
