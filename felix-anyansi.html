[chairman Felix Anyansi explained his leadership’s decision to bring back Paul Aigbogun ahead of the new 2018 Nigerian season] There have been strong speculations concerning our coaching staff, lots of names have been thrown, but Enyimba has never been an organisation to conduct our operations on the pages of newspapers or on social media.

We are a very meticulous club that works diligently and quietly to achieve only the best results. We have appointed Coach Paul and have made the announcement now, so too would we make other announcements at the appropriate times.

I urge our fans to be patient and understanding. We are working very hard, as we always do, to ensure next season is a successful one for all of us.
