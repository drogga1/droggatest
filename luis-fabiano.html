Job Brazillian Footballer
Desc He may not have had the finesse and touch of O Fenomeno but Luis Fabiano will be remembered fondly among Brazil fans as a striker who overcame setbacks to maximise his abilities on the biggest stage for the national side. A prolific striker in his native Brazil with Sao Paulo, Fabiano flopped badly at Porto, scoring just three times in 22 league games. But he wasn’t about to take the easy way out and return to his homeland, instead opting to take up the offer of a move to Sevilla. The Andalusian club has a great track record when it comes to helping Brazilians adapt to life in Europe and has made a habit of rejuvenating waifs and strays following disappointing spells elsewhere.
