Ruud Gullit
Gender Male
Ethnic Dutch
Job Dutch Footballer
Desc A star for AC Milan and the Netherlands in the late 1980s, Ruud Gullit became a cult Premier League icon when he joined Chelsea in 1995. The towering Dutch midfielder had an instant impact at Stamford Bridge, earning a place in the PFA Team of the Year and being crowned the club’s Player of the Year in his first season. He would later go on to manage the club, steering them to FA Cup glory in 1997
