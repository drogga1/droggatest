Omar Sivori
Gender Male
Ethnic Argentinian
Job Argentinian Footballer
Desc 1961 Ballon d’Or winner Omar Sivori was the driving force behind Juventus’ emergence as a true European powerhouse during the 50 and 60s. The Argentinean - who also briefly represented Italy - made over 200 appearances for I Bianconeri, scoring 161 goals while winning three Serie A titles and two Coppa Italias. His move to Juventus came at a cost, however, as he was never selected for Argentina again
