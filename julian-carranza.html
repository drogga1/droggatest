[Inter Miami’s young star Julian Carranza says Beckham’s a cool guy] I thought he was going to be more closed-off because he is such a big star. But nothing could be further from the truth. I found a really cool guy, who wants to work. His office is being finished and he is making his mark. You can see it in the stadium and in training.

So far, nothing. But he’s in good shape, he’ll never lose that right foot, ever. I went in to exercise on our day off and he was there playing with El Chino [goalkeeping coach Sebastian] Saja and his kids. He’s a star

[Beckham is reportedly interested in bringing Cristiano Ronaldo and Lionel Messi to Miami] Ronaldo? Obviously he’ll have the doors open to him if he wants to join up. I have heard more about Messi, that was quite the hot topic around here. If Messi is coming, I’ll be staying here the rest of my life. If I am wanted, I won’t be thinking about going anywhere else!
