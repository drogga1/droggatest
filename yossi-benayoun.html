Desc In his prime, Yossi Benayoun was one of the most flamboyant yet underrated footballers in the Premier League. The 102-time capped former Israel international had spells for clubs like West Ham United, Liverpool, Chelsea, Arsenal and Queens Park Rangers but his best years were certainly for Liverpool during the Reds’ Rafael Benitez era

2020 06 03 Retrieve

[Yossi Benayoun has credited Jurgen Klopp for reviving the glory days at Liverpool] I’m happy to see it, it’s amazing to see where Liverpool are at the moment. From what I see, they play amazing football. The most special thing is you feel they are a team - everyone knows what they’re doing on the pitch, you don’t have one or two big stars and everything is on them. The star of the team is the team itself, one unit. They play together. And the commitment the players have is something special. I don’t see any other team in Europe that has this commitment from the goalkeeper to the striker to work together, to press. When they decide to press they press everyone, no-one can play against them. It’s amazing. Of course, big credit to the manager, to Jurgen Klopp. I think this is the big difference from years before. I really enjoy watching them. Hopefully, they will continue like this and make us proud
