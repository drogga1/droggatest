[Barcelona icon Carles Puyol ‘convinced’ Guardiola will win the Champions League again] For me, Pep is the best coach I had and the best coach in the world. Barcelona and Madrid always have great teams with extraordinary players. Still, it’s correct that they haven’t played as consistently as we’re used to. Both have been eliminated from the Copa del Rey, so there are only two chances left to win a title. The decisive weeks of the season have arrived with the knockout stages in the Champions League, the Clasico and the final stages of La Liga. We will see what answers the teams come up with for the challenges that await. Of course, I hope Barca will win it, but at the moment everything is wide open
