2020 01 23 Retrieve

[Inter have confirmed the signing of Victor Moses from Chelsea after his loan spell with Fenerbahce was brought to an abrupt end] It’s an honour for me to be here and be an Inter player. I’d like to thank everyone for the welcome I’ve been given, I can’t wait to start

[Commenting on his reunion with Conte] Having the opportunity to work with Antonio Conte again is something I’m really happy about. I’ve spoken to him and he’s already explained the club’s project to me. I want to be part of it, I’m happy to be here. I’ll give my all and want to help the team. There’s a lot of quality here, I want to make my contribution out on the pitch and enjoy myself. That’s the most important thing

[On the city of Milan] It seems like a nice city, I’ve liked what I’ve seen up to now. I think I’ll enjoy it here and we’ll have a great future together. I have positive feelings. I’m going to need time to find my feet but I want to help the team win as many games as possible. We’ll do all that we can to achieve our objectives and make our fans proud
