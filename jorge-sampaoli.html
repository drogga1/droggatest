2019 06 14 Retrieve

[Jorge Sampaoli has warned the Real Madrid new boy Rodrygo to expect a ‘difficult’ start to life among ‘icons’ at the Santiago Bernabeu] Rodrygo is going to one of the best teams in the world at a very young age and there will be a very big contrast when he arrives at Real Madrid as he will share a dressing room with players who are very successful and who are icons in Europe. At the beginning, it will be very difficult for him but he has the potential [to make it]. He is very young and he should have the same expectations about his performances after this huge leap he made in his life and his career. In Spain, there is a lot of collective play while here in Brazil these types of players tend to be much more individualistic. During this period, we insisted on the connection of communicating through passing the ball, but also how to pick the moments, to understand when you have to dribble or when to play into space. These are things that you are forced to do in Spain.
