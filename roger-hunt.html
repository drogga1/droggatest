[England legend Roger Hunt criticises Rafael Benitez’s rotation policy at Liverpool] I have been a big fan of Benitez but I have told him that he makes too many changes. There is nothing worse for a player than playing well and yet knowing that you are going to be subbed. Call me old-fashioned, but I believe you play your best team all the time: that is what Bill Shankly used to do. It will be even harder now to get back into the top four, especially with teams such as Tottenham and Manchester City. It won’t be easy for Liverpool
