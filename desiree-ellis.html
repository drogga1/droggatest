[Desiree Ellis on being named the new head coach of the South Africa women’s national team] It still sounds unreal I honestly can’t believe it is really happening. It has always been a dream of mine to be the head coach of Banyana Banyana. Nothing comes bigger than this. I have always believed, and now it is a reality. I will do the best of my ability to make sure we continue on growing this beautiful game. I believe with South Africa behind Banyana Banyana, 2018 is going to be a great year for all of us. We have a lot of work ahead, but I am confident we will get to our destination, which is qualifying for the World Cup in France
