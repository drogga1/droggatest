Jose Santamaria
Gender Male
Ethnic Uruguayan
Job Uruguayan Footballer
Desc Santamaria enjoyed a storied career that took in a multitude of titles at both Nacional in Uruguay, as well as with Real Madrid. Born in Montevideo to Spanish immigrants who’d moved to South America at the turn of the century, Santamaria earned 20 caps for La Celeste and reached the 1954 World Cup semi-finals before earning a further 16 caps for Spain and featuring at the 1962 World Cup for La Roja following his move to Real in 1957
