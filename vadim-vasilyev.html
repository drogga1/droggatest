[Monaco vice president Vadim Vasilyev has admitted that his side cannot hope to compete on a regular basis with Ligue 1 behemoth Paris Saint-Germain] We’re the first club behind this monster. There are always things to improve. We are ambitious and we always want to do better. But I think that the results of the last five years and two world records in the transfer market show everyone that our model is working well

The training centre is in the DNA of our club.
