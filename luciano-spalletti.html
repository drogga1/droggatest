2020 05 18 Retrieve

[Spalletti on his relationship with Totti at Roma] My first Roma team was touched by the light of the Roman sunshine, made up of players who could pass the ball without ever creating difficulty for their team-mates. It takes a lot of quality to do that and they had it, as those passing moves made the difference and formed a team that played beautiful football. What I do is talk to the players, find out what their strengths are and make them the protagonists. I tried to find the hidden capabilities of these players. At the time, I had the zero in front, who was Totti, who’d create spaces and make sure he always got away from the opposition defenders. He was a master in occupying the space

I think our rapport remained the same, but the two phases required a different attitude. The results of the team are the most important thing for a coach and I have to work on that. I have always been the same, it’s just that different things happened requiring me to behave differently, but always for the good of the team. I hope that Totti has a great career as an agent and I am sure we’ll cross paths in future during our work
