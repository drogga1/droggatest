Desc The Bull was signed by Everton for a then-record £3 million after a successful 1994 World Cup with Nigeria, but he endured two frustrating years on Merseyside. Often dealing with injuries and prolonged goal droughts, Amokachi didn’t live up to the expectation of the Toffees while he also struggled to break the partnership of Duncan Ferguson and Paul Rideout up front. Still, his most iconic moment, substituting himself on in Everton’s 4-1 FA Cup semi-final win over Tottenham Hotspur remains a memorable moment in an otherwise dour time in English football
