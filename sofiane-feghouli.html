[Sofiane Feghouli talks about the importance of the FIFA World Cup] I think the World Cup is the very best competition in terms of the feelings and emotions that it generates. We played a historic tie against Germany in which we were on the verge of knocking them out. I was very happy and proud to take part in that incredible adventure. I’d like to play in it again in 2018, although it will be very difficult to qualify
