What we think about is only how to do our best regardless of the result that comes. 2017 03 28 Retrieve

[UAE coach Mahdi Ali announces resignation after Australia defeat] For me, I have spent around 13 years with the national team, and five years with the first team. We have had so many achievements with the team, we have worked very hard and I think this is the time for me to leave.

I tried my best, I did not complain, but now is the time for me to leave. Actually I resigned before the Iraq game, but the federation insisted I continue. I stayed. We won the Iraq game and we had a better chance. I think, after five years, I did my best. We have had so many achievements, winning the Gulf Cup and qualifying to the third round of the World Cup [qualifiers

Given the conditions we have, I don’t think we can achieve better than that. It is a very bad moment for me. I wish I could leave the national team in a better condition. But this is life. We have to accept it. I apologise to all the UAE fans, the leaders, the players, the staff, I thank them all. This is the time for me to leave
