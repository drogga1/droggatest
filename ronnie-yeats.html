[Why do Liverpool wear red & have they always had that colour for their home kit?] We’d just finished training and I was on the way home when Shanks called me back. My initial reaction was that I’d done something wrong. Either that or I was getting a pay rise! Anyway, it was neither. ‘I want you to try on this all-red strip,’ he asked. So I went into the dressing room and put it on

[The switch paid off, with St John netting the opener after just 10 minutes with the game ending in a 3-0 home victory for the Reds] I went home that night.
