[After being named in the NxGn as one of the world’s 50 best teenagers born in 1998 or after] I’m doing well and I try to handle the situation with normality. I assume that my job is to play for the youth team, even though there may be times when I am with the first team. I’m delighted with the situation, working very hard and without thinking about that goal I scored. I prefer to go game by game, without reading the press or taking much notice of what people say. I just want to go day by day, helping my team-mates and receiving their support. I think that is the right path to ensure that things work out well.

[Alena is left-footed, but is currently playing on the right side of the midfield for Barca B] That’s where I like playing best - as an ‘interior’ a pierna cambiada [on the other foot], so that I can cut inside. I try to feel good wherever I play, but if you ask me, that’s the position I like the best

[On Diego Maradona] They don’t say that so much now. It’s something that started in the cadete team because I was left-footed and I had the same hairstyle, but I don’t identify myself with him when I’m playing. I don’t have the same profile as him, but I always took it as a compliment. In any case, I don’t think we can be compared!

Rivaldo was my idol when I was little. Then there was Deco, who played in my position. Now I look a lot at Andres Iniesta, just like I did with Xavi before. That’s the type of profile that interests me, in order to learn and translate that on the field of play.

I want to stay here and reach the first team - that’s clear. But in football you never know. It’s a world in which a lot of things change and I know I could end up somewhere else. But my objective is to be here for many more years

[Barcelona’s primary objective is to win the Champions League this season] Yes, it is clear that the Champions League is a thorn that is stuck in our side. You cannot forget La Liga because every year that we’ve won the Champions League, we’ve also won that. You cannot let go of it because it is the most important and the most regular competition, there is also the Copa del Rey, but the Champions League is the main objective of the dressing room
