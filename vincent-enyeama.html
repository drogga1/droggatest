Desc A long-time teammate of Mikel’s with the Super Eagles, the duo played together with the national side for the best part of the decade. They were key figures of the Nigerian set-up during that tenure, helping the West African transition away from the dying embers of the Golden Generation before ushering in the new era under Stephen Keshi. The pair featured together at the 2014 World Cup, as Nigeria reached the Last 16, and were a part of four Nations Cups squads together-memorably winning the Afcon in 2013
