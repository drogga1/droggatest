2013 11 02 Retrieve

[Fatih Terim on discovering he’d been dismissed as Galatasaray coach] I went to my room shortly after training and learned I’d been sacked through the media! I’ve never seen anything like this in my life

2015 12 13 Retrieve

[Fatih Terim has promised that Turkey will play without fear at Euro 2016 after being drawn against Spain, Croatia and Czech Republic] We will not have fear from anyone and try to enjoy our football on the pitch. There’s no need to talk about Spain. The team is full of stars.

Czech Republic coach Pavel Vrba had told me ‘see you in Paris’ when we last met. We met and we will meet again. He reminded me this just a few minutes ago. Everybody knows about the Czechs. Croatia are our opponents in our 2018 World Cup qualifying campaign too and the first test will be there. I am fully confident of my players. I believe we can do very interesting work there. We can introduce new starlets

2016 06 12 Retrieve

[Fatih Terim, the Turkey coach, has had Emre Mor in his sights] After watching his videos I said he is not an ordinary player, he is an extraordinary player. I think that we made a good decision by calling him up. I insist on saying that he has to learn many things and physically he has to improve. He has the capability of becoming a special footballer. He made the decision to move to Borussia Dortmund and I think he made a good choice

2016 06 17 Retrieve

[Arda Turan’s static showing was in stark contrast to that of Real Madrid midfielder Luka Modric, whose wonderful volley claimed all three points for Croatia] Some players need to make more effort. I expected better from them.

2016 06 21 Retrieve

[Defeats to Croatia and Spain in their first two Group D matches have left Fatih Terim’s side on the brink] I believe in my players. This is the last step, the last leg. It will be either ‘yes’ or ‘no’. We will either say ‘enough’ or ‘keep going.
