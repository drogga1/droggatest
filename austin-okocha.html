[Former Nigeria captain Austin Okocha has picked former Eintracht Frankfurt teammate Tony Yeboah as his best Ghanaian player of all time] My best Ghanaian footballer of all-time is Tony Yeboah. Tony was a great guy. I have played with lots of players but Tony was exceptional, he always [conjures some] magic even when there is no hope. With Tony in the team, you are assured of a goal in every match. He will just pop up out of [nowhere] and get you a goal. I played with so many players during my career, but I remember when I signed for Frankfurt, that’s where I first witnessed pure magic from an African player. He was so great that in Germany, instead of Jehovah’s Witness, they called him Yegoala witness. I know Ghana have produced so many great players in the past but in my opinion, Tony Yeboah stands tall
