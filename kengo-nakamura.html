Kengo Nakamura
Gender Male
Ethnic Japanese
Job Japanese Footballer
Desc Nakamura is one of the most popular players in the J-League. A full university graduate, he joined Frontale aged 22, and for the last eight-and-a-half years, he has been the symbol of the Kawasaki club. Kengo, as he is known to distinguish him from ex-Celtic man Shunsuke Nakamura, is a midfielder who specialises in distributing the ball and setting the tempo for the team. A quiet leader, he can cover any role in midfield and is gifted with a very powerful and precise shot with both feet
