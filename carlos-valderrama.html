Carlos Valderrama
Spellling El Pibe
Gender Male
Ethnic Colombian
Job Colombian Footballer
Desc He’s remembered for his hair more than anything else, which is a shame considering how important Carlos Valderrama was for Colombian football during the 80s and 90s. A deep-lying playmaker with a keen eye for a raking pass, Valderrama was far from your typical number 10. He wasn’t necessarily that prolific in the final third, but El Bibe’s tactical intelligence and positional awareness ensured that he remained a fixture in the Colombian setup for 13 years between 1985 and 1998

[Carlos Valderrama said he will cut his famous hair if Colombia win the World Cup] If we are world champions … it’s yes. We don’t qualify for World Cups very often, you know. So, we have to use this opportunity and if we reach there, we live it in the flesh

[Colombia legend Carlos Alberto Valderrama Palacio, also known as El Pibe, says a local coach is better to lead the La Tricolor] Without a doubt, the coach must be Colombian. We have trained coaches to lead the national team.
