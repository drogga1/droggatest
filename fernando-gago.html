[Barcelona talisman Lionel Messi is capable of winning a game in half a second] We know that he has the ability to win a match in half a second and that gives you peace of mind. I have no resemblance to Fernando [Redondo] and that was for the journalists and not me. He is my idol but there was never a possible comparison.
