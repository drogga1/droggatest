[Ozil didn’t want to commit to Erdogan’s policies] I do not think that Ozil deliberately wanted to commit to Erdogan’s policies. Above all, the timing was unfortunate considering what is happening politically in Germany. For me, football is football and politics is politics. If both are mixed, something like this can happen. If there is a problem between two parties, the one who doesn’t talk always loses. Then if you talk about it, it’s too late.
