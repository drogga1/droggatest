2019 07 01 Retrieve

[Fabian Ruiz cited Spain’s togetherness in overcoming adversity as a key factor in their European Under-21 Championship triumph] I’m delighted to have won the award but winning the tournament was more important. We started badly against Italy, and things were difficult. We had to beat Poland by more than three goals. But the side was confident, and we deserved to be champions. The key has been to be together - we’ve got a great team spirit. We had to give our best. Germany are a great side and we had to suffer together. They’re great players but the 2-0 [advantage] gave us peace of mind and confidence and I think we are deserved winners.
