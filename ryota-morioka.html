Ryota Morioka
Gender Male
Ethnic Japanese
Job Japanese Footballer
Desc Promising youngster Morioka has started for Vissel on more than one occasion this season. He is the product of Kumiyama High School, one of the top football schools in Japan, famous for its passing style. Morioka likes to play between the opposition’s last two lines and to move across the width of the field. His specialties are very precise and quick vertical passes, to set his team-mates in front of the goal. There’s still a lot of room to improve for him, but he is absolutely one of the most skilled players of his generation
