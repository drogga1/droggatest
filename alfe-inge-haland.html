There is no doubt the Premier League is attractive to him. But we must wait and see if he gets a chance

They have thought for a long time that they would give him the chance this summer, and at the same time there are many things that have taken place. Most of the job he has done himself, but he has been assisted by his former team-mates and current coaches and team-mates, in addition to the hard work he has put in over many years

[father Alf-Inge says Man Utd-linked Haaland interested in future Premier League move] He was a Leeds fan and a [Manchester] City fan and a Nottingham Forest fan.
