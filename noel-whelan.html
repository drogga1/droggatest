2019 12 19 Retrieve

[Noel Whelan says Jurgen Klopp will be keen for Rhian Brewster to join Leeds] There is obviously interest from Leeds in Brewster and I’m sure there are a queue of clubs wanting to take him. But Leeds have a good chance, definitely. A huge thing in Leeds’ favour is that Marcelo Bielsa is a great coach and Leeds play in a similar way to Liverpool - build out from the back, press from the front, high energy, smooth passing combinations and an aggressive playing style. That’s how Leeds signed Jack Harrison from Man City. Jurgen Klopp will be aware of that, and how Harrison has developed under Bielsa. Klopp will be keen for Brewster to join Leeds and learn under Bielsa. It’s probably in Klopp’s mind to send him to a team that play a similar style of football to Liverpool, and that’s where Leeds will fit the bill. The issue is how much game time he would have, as Patrick Bamford is the number one centre-forward at Leeds
