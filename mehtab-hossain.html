2017 04 08 Retrieve

[Mohun Bagan plan on spoiling East Bengal’s Mehtab Hossain’s potential last Kolkata Derby] Every derby is important for me. Of course, life won’t end for me but every player has to leave a club someday or the other. Yes, I had told that if we won’t win the league, I will leave East Bengal. Players are scrutinised when they don’t perform well and that has been the case for us after our lull following a good start to the league. If we win the I-League, I will continue here at East Bengal, else I will walk away.

We have been getting close to winning the league for the last seven to eight years but if I walk away and someone else gets the opportunity to win, no one else will be more happy than me. This has been my family for the last ten years

2017 07 04 Retrieve

[2016 Indian Super League runners-up Kerala Blasters have not retained Mehtab Hossain and the midfielder is all set to join the draft later this month] Yes, it is true that Kerala Blasters had contacted me and I had spoken to the owners. But, I chose not to give it another go and instead go into the draft. Let’s see what the future holds.

East Bengal is a club close to my heart and there was an offer too but my words have some value too. I had always said that I would leave them if we did not win and so I did
