Marko Marin
Gender Male
Ethnic German
Job German Footballer
Desc Marko Marin has had a nomadic career since breaking through in Germany with Borussia Monchengladbach after turning 18. He earned a big-money move to Werder Bremen before signing for Chelsea in 2012. His performances for the Blues were very inconsistent and resulted in multiple loan spells, in four different countries. Two more countries have since followed, with Marin permanently moving to Olympiacos in Greece and most recently Red Star Belgrade in Serbia.
