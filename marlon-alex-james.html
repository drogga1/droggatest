2013 01 03 Retrieve

[As a result, ATM gained promotion back into the Super League, and with kick off the new season edging closer, Marlon is still confident of haunting defenders] I do not set personal targets. What is important is the performance of my team. Of course, as a striker, my job is to find the net as many times as possible. I had a great season in 2012 […] I want to make it better in the upcoming season. Yes, at 36, I am classified as an old man but as long as I can score goals against younger players, I will continue to haunt them. I do not want to think about the other teams. It will only make me nervous. I am, however, aware we will face a tougher competition in top-tier football. I only want to think about Forces. A few key players have left but we have beefed-up our squad with quality players. I am confident we will be very competitive

2013 10 23 Retrieve

[ATM striker Marlon Alex James remains unfazed ahead of the second leg of the 2013 Malaysia Cup semifinal against Kelantan in Kota Bharu this Friday] I don’t really study my opponents before matches. I just make sure that I play my ‘A’ game. Obinna is a good player and football is not just about beating a player to win the match but we have to beat the entire Kelantan team

[Marlon, who is a joint-second top scorer in the competition with seven goals, also debunked the assumption that the Gladiators are a two-man team that heavily depend on him and his strike partner and skipper Hairuddin Omar] It’s quite disrespectful to brand us a two-man team as all the players work hard and we have always played as a unit. We will go out for a win and we will play an attacking game, which is what we have done throughout the season. We conceded early in the first leg and it’s something we can’t afford to do in the return leg

[Nonetheless, Marlon is well aware of the threat that the Red Warriors possess, especially on their home ground] Kelantan were the best team last season in terms of consistency but I believe we were the best football playing team. The tie is far from over and we are targeting an upset in Kota Baru.
