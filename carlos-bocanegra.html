2018 01 17 Retrieve

[Why many talented international players won’t be drafted by MLS teams] The landscape is changing. A lot of the good kids coming up through the academies are being signed to a homegrown contract or they’re signed to a USL team through the club. It’s just the evolution of where we are as a country with this combine and draft.

For me, I like to just look at the top players, and afterwards worry about whether they’re foreign or not. With [Julian] Gressel, we thought about it and we had availability with our roster construction. For us, last year he was the most pro-ready guy at that pick, and it ended turning out that he was.

I actually think that it wouldn’t be a good thing [to have the draft be filled with Americans] because then it would mean we’re not getting the American kids into the pro game at an earlier age to get them in those environments. I think this is a good sign that you’re not seeing as many American players in this combine and in the draft because they’re going the homegrown and USL route, and they’re in that professional environment at an earlier age

[Former USMNT captain Carlos Bocanegra on Aaron Long] The career path that he’s had to get to where he is is incredible so it’s fun to root for him. I remember seeing him before he had that terrible mullet. It’s nice to see him with the national team, especially when he captained the country. That was pretty cool.

He’s hard to beat one on one. The kid just doesn’t stop. I remember a few times when he came back against (Atlanta United), chasing down Josef Martinez or whatever, he makes himself hard to beat. He’s just got that grit and determination

2020 01 22 Retrieve

[Atlanta United traded Julian Gressel to D.C. United on Tuesday] We knew we were going to get absolutely hammered by the fan base. We knew this was coming. But again, with Julian, he’s a great kid and I don’t think this would’ve been a good situation for anyone. Julian would be playing not to get hurt, he’d be worrying about his future. Again, we could’ve made him a bona fide offer and we’d have retained his rights within MLS in perpetuity. It was coming down to a situation where nobody really wanted it to go. There was a solution where we felt we were getting a good return for him. We can utilize those funds and it will go into our greater plan and Julian will get a good situation as well, he gets a resolution and he’s happy

[Bocanegra said of Gressel] He’s a fan favorite, that’s not lost on us. We get this, that’s a difficult decision and we understand that the fan base is going to be asking a lot of questions about this. And that’s not something we take lightly. But in this business you’ve got to try and do what’s best for the club and the player as well
