2019 08 19 Retrieve

[Fiorentina sporting director Daniele Prade says he would like to sign Franck Ribery] We really like Ribery, but like all great players, they are experiencing a moment of reflection. We would like to have him with us, but he has very important financial offers and we cannot guarantee him tens of millions like the Arab or Russian teams. But if he wanted to continue playing at high levels, Florence and Fiorentina would wait for him with open arms. At this moment the deal is not close, there has been no progress. If he doesn’t come, we will buy another player

[Fiorentina’s sporting director Daniele Prade has claimed that Sporting midfielder Bruno Fernandes is on his way to Real Madrid] Raphinha must be off the market because Bruno Fernandes is off to Real Madrid for €70 million.

2019 09 03 Retrieve

[Sporting director Daniele Prade has confirmed Suso and Matteo Politano were among a host of players Fiorentina attempted to sign on transfer deadline day] I’m not disappointed by any of our negotiations. We tried until 8pm last night, but the clubs didn’t want to sell, so I’m sure I can’t do more. Sassuolo wouldn’t sell Domenico Berardi, and it was the same with Udinese and Rodrigo De Paul, Inter and Matteo Politano, and Milan and Suso. But we tried. Raphinha is the only one we let go because the costs were too high for us

[On De Paul] I have an excellent relationship with [Udinese owner] Giampaolo Pozzo and we didn’t want to force it. I never talked to De Paul, because we never found the agreement with Udinese. Other claims are false

[Nainggolan left Inter for former employers Cagliari on loan] With Radja Nainggolan, we never got to talk to him. It is right that he went to Cagliari. As for Daniele De Rossi, I knew from the beginning that he would never have played in Italy with another team. He made the best choice for his life
