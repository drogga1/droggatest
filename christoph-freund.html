2020 06 08 Retrieve

[Red Bull Salzburg sporting director Christoph Freund says the Austrian side are not worried about losing coach Jesse Marsch to Borussia Dortmund] I have a contract here and my focus is 100 per cent here in Salzburg. It’s not necessary to speak about it, and I have to say that I haven’t been in contact with Dortmund. It is nice when it is rumored that a coach we have will go to such a large club. It is not currently an issue. Jesse has a long-term contract, the decision is clearly up to us. No one has spoken to him yet.

[Freund believes the latest rumours shows his club have hired the right people in recent seasons] This confirms that we have good coaches. Marco Rose wasn’t that big of a name before, Adi Hutter is doing an outstanding job, [Wolfsburg coach] Oliver Glasner was our assistant coach and now Jesse has already made a name for himself. I think that’s a cool story
