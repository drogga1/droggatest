[Toronto FC’s Sebastian Giovinco has given his backing to former club Juventus] I think it might be their season. Moreover, in the summer they have improved by signing important players. [Major League Soccer] is improving, even thought it remains to be seen how much it wants to keep improving. I think about going back to Italy sometimes, that’s normal. When you’re away from Italy you miss it in a general sense. I still have a year on my contract in Toronto and I’m very happy living this experience
