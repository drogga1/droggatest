[Paul Leary, Chairman of Marine AFC, a seventh-tier club in England which hosted a Borussia Dortmund training session on the morning of their UEFA Europa League match against Liverpool] When the e-mail landed from Borussia Dortmund, it was 1 April. We got the request asking if they could use our facilities for a training session on the morning of the game, and I had to say look, if this is an April Fools’ joke then stop now. But it’s great for the club and the local area, to have these players here. They did an hour on the pitch, the full squad, and then off they went. I did ask them if they could maybe loan us a couple of players for our next crucial league game, but I had to make do with a signed shirt!
