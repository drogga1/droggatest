[Javi Gracia signed a four-and-a-half-year contract in November 2018] Yes, it’s something that goes out of the ordinary. It is not usual to renew the contract as I did for four more years. What it does is reinforce you, not only internally but also externally. It gives you an authority in front of players, and they will say, ‘hey, this coach is going to be here longer, it’s not going to be like the previous ones that have been a short cycle’.
