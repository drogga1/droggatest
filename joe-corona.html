[LA Galaxy midfielder Joe Corona says that it was a privilege to play alongside Zlatan Ibrahimovic] Zlatan is a personality. Unique. He had his moments like we all do. From my point of view, it was a privilege to play alongside a player who’s a soccer legend. To be his teammate you have stories that you remember and you can laugh about

[Even in victory, the Swedish star always called for more from his LA Galaxy teammates] I think it was a game that we won, something like 3-0. The game was over and he came in and scolded all of us. That’s how he was and that’s what his personality was. On a personal level I never had a problem with him. On the field he demanded a lot. But we always had a great relationship.
