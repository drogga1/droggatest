[Tigres UANL winger Jurgen Damm: Borussia Dortmund and Wolfsburg were interested in me] My agent had close contact with Borussia Dortmund and Wolfsburg - they were the two teams from Germany that were interested. The issue was the release clause, which was very high. As I have said on many occasions, the price of Mexican players is very inflated. Of course, Borussia or Wolfsburg can pay 10 or 15 million [euros], but it’s not a matter of having it but of what you are really worth.

[Atlanta United have been credited with an interest in Damm] It is an option that we manage for my future. MLS is a very attractive league that has grown a lot in recent years. I do not shut off any options, I just wouldn’t want to play for River and Monterrey, because I am a Tigres fan

In Argentina I like Boca a lot more, aside from us losing the Copa Libertadores final [to River]. Tigres is the team of my heart, the one I love, the one I appreciate and will be a fan off all my life
