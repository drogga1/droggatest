[Assistant head coach of Penang, Darren Reid was clearly dissapointed with this team for wasting a lot of chances after their 1-0 defeat to T-Team] The boys gave their heart and soul with their performance, but goals win games, and if you don’t take your chances, you are never going to win the game.
