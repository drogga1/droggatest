[Robert Lewandowski is not irreplaceable for Poland] It’s true that he is a very great player. It’s not impossible that the one who takes his place does as well, if not better. We cannot know it.

[Szarmach has called on his nation to go far in the World Cup] Why not? In football, anything is possible. There is not really a favourite. Poland can very well go to the final and why not win it.

[Poland finishing third at the 1974 World Cup] It’s true we were not far away. We could have won it, the first time in 1974. We played the game [against West Germany] with difficult conditions. There was a lot of water, the pitch was soaked. We could have done better that’s true, but nevertheless we were happy
