[Ademola Lookman decided to reject a loan move to Championship club Derby County in favour of Bundesliga side RB Leipzig] I wanted a new challenge, a new league. The Bundesliga has a great reputation in England. Top stadiums with good, physically strong and disciplined players and games played at a high level. The training ground could not be better. The training center leaves nothing to be desired - it is all highly professional. The food in the academy tastes like a top restaurant!
