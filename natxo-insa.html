[Who is JDT’s new signing Natxo Insa] Insa is a box-to-box midfielder and he was a regular starter in the Levante side that dominated the Second Division this season. There is not much we can say about his attributes and playing style as he has played most of his career in that category. He had a decent showing in his lone First Division season with Celta five years ago, but the team opted not to renew his contract and he went to the Turkish league, where he didn’t have much success. He is a bit of a nomad that never spents more than two seasons in the same team and he just left a goodbye message for Levante in which he siad he is leaving because he didn’t felt truly appreciated by the team.
