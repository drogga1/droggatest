Phillip Cocu
Gender Male
Ethnic Dutch
Job Dutch Footballer
Desc Barcelona beat competition from Real Madrid, Juventus and Inter among others for the signature of free agent Phillip Cocu in 1998 and the Dutch midfielder became a mainstay. Cocu made a total of 291 appearances of the Catalan club, which was a record until Lionel Messi surpassed it in 2012. Unfortunately for the Netherlands international, his time at Camp Nou coincided with a lean period at the club as he collected just one Liga title in six seasons.
