2018 10 11 Retrieve

[Paris Saint-Germain’s Germany international Thilo Kehrer has spoken in glowing terms about Kylian Mbappe and Neymar] Of course, I have got to know [Mbappe] quite differently as a team-mate. As a player, he has outstanding ability, but at the same time he is a very down to earth, funny guy in direct contact. Neymar is a sociable and nice person. The way he carries himself privately is quite different to how he may come across in the media. In the dressing room, we have a very good mood, which has made it really easy for me so far. It worked very well, I feel well in Paris.
