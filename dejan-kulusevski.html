[Kulusevski credits his parents with playing a pivotal role in his meteoric rise] I know that when I start thinking too much, I play badly. So, I try to be like when I was a child in Sweden, where I only thought about having fun. When I have fun, I’m strong

2020 05 27 Retrieve

[New Juventus signing Dejan Kulusevski says he chose a move to Turin over Inter for tactical reasons] Exactly. With Conte, the movements are different in terms of the tactical set-up. And I had doubts about that. Sarri was nice, he put me at ease. I knew his Chelsea team and his style of play. This is also why I chose Juventus

[Speaking about the day he signed for Juve] What a thrill that day at the stadium was - a unique atmosphere. And Ronaldo said to me ‘welcome Dejan, how are you?’ (Gianluigi) Buffon and (Giorgio) Chiellini were also nice to me. It was an immense joy for a boy who had only seen them on TV. I can’t wait to link up with (Paulo) Dybala and his magical left foot. In any case, I now know how to play in multiple roles and I plan to carve out a spot for myself in Turin too.

[Kulusevski is grateful to Parma for giving him the chance of regular first-team football] In the summer, when the sporting director Faggiano and (Parma head coach Roberto) D’Aversa offered me this opportunity, they showed great confidence in me. It has been an extraordinary season for me and I want to close it well

My debut at San Siro against Inter - a magnificent stadium. I looked calm but at half-time I threw up from the stress. It was an unforgettable 2-2 draw

2020 05 28 Retrieve

[Kulusevski reveals why he copied Man Utd flop’s squad number] I was in Sweden to see Manchester United and I witnessed Januzaj’s debut. He entered the pitch with an incredible personality, even though it was his first professional game. I said to my dad that I wanted to be like that, I wanted play that way. I got attached to that number, read a few things and fell in love

[on Kevin De Bruyne] He is one of the top five players in the world. I study him a lot because he is a phenomenal player and because I want to become like him. One of my habits is to watch many games, because I learn a lot from my colleagues
