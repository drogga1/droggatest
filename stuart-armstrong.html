It’s always hard when you’re not playing games, not starting games especially, because nobody likes sitting on the bench. I’m not the most patient. I need to learn to adapt. I would say it’s a different type of challenge. If I’d stayed at Celtic and continued to have success I’d view my career when it finished as a very good one and a very successful one. I think each individual is different, they’ll have their own desires and want to have their own experiences. I grew up watching a lot of Premier League - it’s something I dreamed about as a young player. That was my own interest and something I’m very happy to be experiencing right now
