Desc Kondogbia was another midfield recruit for Monaco in 2013, arriving on the back of an impressive campaign with Sevilla. The French-born youngster went on to make 49 appearances in two years at the club before joining Inter in 2015. His fortunes in the San Siro were mixed. After making 43 league starts across two campaigns, he moved to Valencia on loan with Joao Cancelo going the other way. After four goals and as many assists in 31 La Liga appearances, Valencia signed him permanently in 2018. Kondogbia helped Los Che to the Copa del Rey in 2019, his first senior trophy.
