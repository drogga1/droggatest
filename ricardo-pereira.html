[Ricardo Pereira: It’s my goal to play at Euro 2020 for Portugal] This is a goal since the start of the season. But I must play very well here with Leicester to have the opportunity to help the national team at the European Championship

[Portugal have been given an unfavourable route to defend their European title, having been drawn in Group F along with France and Germany] It’s a very difficult group. There are three big teams, but I don’t think there are any favorites. We’re all going to play against each other. It’s going to be very tight, for sure. But anything can happen and I hope Portugal will win at the end

[Portugal are European champions but France lifted the World Cup last year to become world champions] Perhaps. If they became world champions, it is not by chance. But Germany also has a good squad. They will be two very difficult games.
