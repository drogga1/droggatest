He’s physical, strong, effective at both ends, whereas Manchester United require a much more technically gifted player than, unfortunately, Fellaini’s capable of. Effective, that’s the word, for his time at Manchester United, but you can understand why Ole Gunnar Solskjaer feels him surplus to requirements and I think for all parties it would be best if he found himself elsewhere. He’s a nice feller. He took a while to learn the language but a really nice feller, desperate to get involved in the dressing room. He really did bring an awful lot to the dressing room as well. He’s one of those payers you thought you could rely on and you could turn to him in a desperate situation. I feel now, even at United, he won’t be one of those players that will ruffle the dressing room and upset the way things are going. I think he will get on with his job as a professional but he will want to play football before his career ends.
