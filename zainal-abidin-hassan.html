2017 04 14 Retrieve

[Zainal urges his team to keep their winning momentum, and warn them about Kelantan in-form striker Mohamed Ghaddar] For me, we must pay attention to Ghaddar, Badhri Razi and Indra Putra. We must not let them to have a shot or any attempt at our goal. Even though we won against Felda United before, it doesnt mean that we can take this game lightly because we know Kelantan play at a really high tempo.

The last two games for the first round of Super League for us are like a do-or-die missions if we want to book a spot in the Malaysia Cup. I am confident that the players will give their 100% focus when we face Kelantan this Saturday and JDT next week
