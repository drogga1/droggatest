Gender Male
Ethnic Brazillian
Job Brazillian Footballer
Desc Perennially linked with Tottenham back in the day, Damiao was the great bright hope of Brazilian football, having first broken through at Internacional in 2009, playing a key role in their 2010 Copa Libertadores success and earning praise from Ronaldo along with a Brazil call-up in the process. By 2012 he was following in the footsteps of Romario and Bebeto by finishing top scorer as Brazil’s no.9 at the London Olympic Games. The Selecao may have ultimately lost out to Mexico in the final but Damiao seemed destined for bigger things
