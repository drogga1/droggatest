[The best right-back in the world? How Trent Alexander-Arnold is reinventing the full-back] There is always a generational time lag. Young kids watch the emergence of Trent Alexander-Arnold and want to emulate him. In our team, playing at full-back isn’t viewed as something especially positive, particularly among classic wingers who could actually play quite well there. We’ve tried to discourage that this season by playing a diamond in midfield, which allows our full-backs to be the main outlet. Kids who can develop those in-between techniques are now at an advantage. Having the transferrable skills is massively important for their long-term progressions
