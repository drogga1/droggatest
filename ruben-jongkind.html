Desc De Ligt is perhaps the most successful case study for Ajax’s ‘Plan Cruyff’, a strategy written and implemented by former head of talent development Ruben Jongkind. It led to the rebuilding of the club after Johan Cruyff returned to the board in 2011 and was nicknamed the ‘Velvet Revolution’. It stated Ajax should play attractive, attacking football, and that at least half the first team should be home-grown players
