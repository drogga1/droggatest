2019 10 23 Publish: Jeremy Stephens says Khabib Nurmagomedov ‘doesn’t have the standup’ to mess with Tony Ferguson

Jeremy Stephens may have never fought Khabib Nurmagomedov, but ‘Lil Heathen’ has plenty of first-hand experience on the mat with Tony Ferguson, and the UFC veteran believes ‘El Cucuy’ is more than capable of handing ‘The Eagle’ his first ever career loss and relieving the undefeated Dagestani of his championship duty

Speaking to Helen Yee earlier this month, Stephens, who recently fought Yair Rodriguez in a Fight of the Night classic at UFC on ESPN 6 (Stephens lost via unanimous decision), said Nurmagomedov doesn’t have the standup to mess with Ferguson on the feet

Khabib is undefeated at 28-0 and believes he should be ranked ahead of Jon Jones as the No. 1 pound-for-pound fighter in the world. Stephens, however, thinks the wrestling powerhouse needs more title defenses to his name before he claims the #1 spot
