2020 07 11 Retrieve

[A stalemate at Oakwell kept Barnsley at the foot of the Championship table on paper and Wigan effectively bottom once their 12-point penalty is applied] I think any point is gold in this league at the minute. The lads worked tirelessly again today and I thought we thoroughly deserved a point. I have so much respect for Barnsley and the other teams in the league so with the competition as it is we were expecting a tough game because they have a lot of young and energetic lads so you have to move the ball nice and brightly and in spells we did that.

Since the turn of the year, we are the in-form team in the league so it is up to us to keep our head down and the lads are giving us everything at the minute. We have a great changing room and the lads have said in interviews how they have each other’s back, they work hard and they cover every blade of grass for each other
