[Neymar’s lucrative salary at Paris Saint-Germain matches the combined earnings of performers across the top seven leagues in the women’s game] We’d love to see more governing bodies valuing their female athletes the same as their male athletes. It’s not just about equal pay; it’s about the message that sends out about how much women are valued.
