Desc Much like Ronaldo, Diego Tardelli’s first foray into European football came in Holland with PSV Eindhoven. But while O Fenomeno dazzled, the Dutch club ditched Tardelli, who returned to Brazil after just three goals in 13 Eredivisie games. Tardelli spent the large bulk of his career back in his homeland, starring with Atletico Mineiro over two spells with money-spinning stints at Anzhi Makhachkala and Al Gharafa sandwiched in between
