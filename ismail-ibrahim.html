2017 04 19 Retrieve

[On top of their must-win AFC Cup match against the Cambodians on Wednesday, JDT will face rivals Pahang in their FA Cup encounter this Sunday] Tomorrow’s [Wednesday] match is a vital game for us. We need three points so the mission tomorrow is we must win. Hopefully tomorrow night the boys will play 100% to get three points.
