[Biancucchi, cousin of Lionel Messi and two years his senior, also put pen to paper on a fresh deal this summer] If it were up to me I would already be at Newell’s, but I have to think about my family, I think the people there [in Argentina] are far too aggressive and that makes you hesitate. [Above all Biancucchi has always strived to make his own way in football] It is simple in football: if you play well you will be applauded, if you play badly you will insulted, be you the cousin of Maradona, Pele or whoever. Leo is a phenomenal player and even more so as a person, but I want to be judged for what I do on the pitch
