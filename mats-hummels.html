[Mats Hummels has stated his support for introducing ‘orange cards’ in football as well as cracking down on time wasting] Timed penalties [off the pitch as punishment] are an option, I think that’s not a bad idea. Of all the proposals, this could be the best to implement

We had some opponents this year which started time wasting in the 15th minute. If referees were willing to show a yellow card after 20 minutes, the issue would have been settled and the player who did it could not do it again. But currently it is often the case that the refs wait until the 87th minute to show a card

A video referee is useful for sure. Some people may say wrong decisions belong in football, but if a final is decided in the 90th minute by a wrong decision, for me that is not a fair result. A mistake decided the game. If we talk about that, I would be delighted If I would know you can watch the replay afterwards. And if [there is not enough evidence] to overturn the decision which was made you just retain the original call. In my eyes that’s quite simple

The shootout [proposal] is an idea, but I don’t know if that would make any difference in contrast to penalties and in a few years’ time we would be thinking about something new again. You could do that, but actually penalties already offer a lot of drama

[On getting rid of offsides] No, that makes no sense
