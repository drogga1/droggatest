Desc Initially signed on loan from Benfica in 2014, the Portuguese playmaker joined for £14million in January 2015. Bernardo soon emerged as a key creative force at Monaco, starring in their triumphant Ligue 1 campaign and run to the Champions League semi-finals. After three successful years in Ligue 1, he joined Manchester City in 2017, helping Pep Guardiola’s team to successive league titles in 2018 and 2019
