Kota Ueda
Gender Male
Ethnic Japanese
Job Japanese Footballer
Desc Ueda has been tipped by many observers as the ‘next Yasuhito Endo,’ but so far he hasn’t caught the attention of Japan boss Alberto Zaccheroni, possibly because a lack of consistency. However, when in good form, Ueda is possibly the best midfielder of his generation in Japan. His main quality is an innate sense of positioning, that helps him both to create and to cover spaces. He is also a set-piece specialist and can deliver perfectly-curved crosses on both free-kicks and corners
