[The 18-year-old recently traveled to Munich] I remember telling our youth coach that Alphonso would play in the first team in two years. I was completely wrong, everything went much faster. I am firmly convinced that FC Bayern is exactly the right club at the right time for Alphonso. The situation is just perfect for him. Bayern has made clear from the beginning what they intend to do with Alphonso. They want to develop him further, but they also want to give it equal deployment time in the first team. Alphonso has to play, that’s crucial. And he still has to face new challenges. I am sure that he will grow and be successful in Munich
