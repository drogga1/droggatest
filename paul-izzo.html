2017 04 19 Retrieve

[Central Coast Mariners have confirmed Paul Izzo will leave the club] It’s very tough for me to leave the Mariners. This season we’ve seen how good things can get at this club and how good they can be so it was a hard decision [to leave]. [Central Coast’s goalkeeping coach] Matt Nash has done so much for me, he never gave up on me and has put so much time and hard work into me. I want to say a huge thanks to the Central Coast Mariners who took a big gamble on me. It took time for me to put in a strong season. They were very patient and showed a lot of faith in me.
