2017 02 21 Retrieve

[New England Revolution hope two-striker look of 2016 can form foundation for 2017 success] It was across the board. I think maybe we got a little stagnant. Maybe we didn’t adapt well enough. Maybe we became too predictable.

When we decided to make the change, it felt like a big change because of how we’d been playing and we’d had such success in the other formation. But in reality, the players took to it really well

We had spent a lot of time working on it, so when we did make the switch, players were revitalized, showing up in a little different positions, creating a little different angles for themselves. The passing lanes became different and we had to be a little tougher defensively

When we played with the diamond, the No. 6 stayed as the No. 6, and really stayed and protected the two center backs. I think we’ll incorporate that more across all formations
