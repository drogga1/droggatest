[Antonio Carlos Zago says he’d pay to play in the Derby della Capitale] I’d pay to play against Lazio. For me it would be a dream. In Rome, the derby has a special meaning. If you win, it can change the season. We’ve had three defeats in the derby, now is the time to get back to winning. Like Totti, I also do not have a great fondness for Lazio. You cannot make a prediction

[Zago went on to reveal that he was overcome with emotion when he got the post as assistant coach of the club] It was an indescribable emotion. When I came back inside I locked myself in the bathroom and cried. The sports culture in Rome has a special meaning. I think a player who has history with Roma cannot play for Lazio. With AC Milan and Inter, they happen to exchange players. It’s always a special game. From the first day I arrived in Rome in 1998, I realised how important the derby is. I think they will suffer from the match played on Thursday in Turkey
