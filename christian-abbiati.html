Everyone who’s been given the chance this season has played his part in Milan’s success. Definitely, this is a big season for us, because the team is doing well and our final objective is so close we can almost touch it. 2011 09 03 Retrieve

[Christian Abbiati says AC Milan have been the cornerstone of success since 2000] Everyone talks about the Barcelona model but we have been the model to follow since 2000 and we don’t even know it. We won the Champions League in 2003 and the year after we slipped up. We were in the final of 2005 and two years later we were European champions again. We are competitive. Last year we won the Scudetto and went out of the Champions League because of a single shot in two games. Only Real and Barcelona are superior to us. For the league, apart from us I see Inter, Napoli and Juventus [competing].
