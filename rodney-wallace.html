[How Ronald Matarrita convinced Rodney Wallace to join NYCFC] [Matarrita] was the first person I called as soon as I found out they had interest and told him the situation. Right away he pushed for it to happen because he’s a player that’s been on the team. They know him and trust him, and have confidence in what he had to say.

[Portland] had interest in me since November, but I was still under contract with [Sport Recife] and was trying to figure out how to get out of that deal. Towards the end, the window of opportunity for that situation just kept getting smaller and smaller and at the same time [the Timbers] were making their moves. This opportunity came about where New York showed interested and for me it was a good opportunity that I wanted to explore. Things just started happening faster with New York, and I was able to get out of my contract with Brazil

Once they reached out, they were pretty consistent and persistent with their approach and their interest. As a player you want to be somewhere where you’re valued, and where they believe in you, and ultimately it felt like New York was that place for me

They’re a team that was close to doing it last year, and now they’ve added more pieces, which just shows their ambition to win. You always want to be a part of that
