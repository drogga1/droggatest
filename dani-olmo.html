[Dani Olmo reveals he received offers from Barca & Premier League clubs before sealing RB Leipzig switch] I had several offers, not only from Spain, but also from Italy and England. Because I wanted to go to a team that had a project now and in the future for me. Leipzig offered it to me and other teams did not.
