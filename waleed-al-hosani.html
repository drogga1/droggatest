2017 08 15 Retrieve

[Acting CEO of the UAE Pro League Committee, Mr. Waleed Al Hosani believes hosting the competition in Egypt for the second year in a row is a testament to the strong ties between the two countries, highlighting the fact that UAE have hosted the Egyptian Super Cup twice as well] The PLC aims to achieve several objectives by holding the Arabian Gulf Super Cup outside the country.
