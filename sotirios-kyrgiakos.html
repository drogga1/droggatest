[Sotirios Kyrgiakos says Impossible to overstate how good Gerrard was] I have many great memories from my career. But I have to say that being signed by Liverpool was something very important for me.

Benitez chose me for the club, and for that I have to thank him. I can say for sure that he was the best manager I worked for. He is very smart, fantastic tactically and also a great person.

It was an honour to play for King Kenny. For me, he and Steven Gerrard are the two greatest players ever to have played for Liverpool. They have played a huge part in the club’s history

It is impossible to overstate how good a footballer Gerrard was. He was such a great player and a great leader. He helped everybody on and off the pitch. I know he will help Rangers a lot. He already has. I hope soon he will win some trophies, because I have no doubt he will make a very good manager too. Rangers is a big football club – the best in Scotland. To play against Celtic in my time there was huge and unique. It is the biggest football derby on the planet, for me.
