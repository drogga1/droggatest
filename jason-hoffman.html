[Newcastle Jets full-back Jason Hoffman rates Sunday’s clash with the Central Coast Mariners as the biggest F3 Derby in recent years] All week in training and come match day, I’m that extra little bit fired up. Being a Newcastle boy, I understanding the history behind it. This is a derby that is important regardless of the ladder situation. That [finals hopes] just adds to the importance of it

Whenever we play the Mariners, whether it’s a pre-season friendly or a league match, they are always massive games. They are important to the playing group and also to the fans. With the ladder position the way it is this is obviously one of the most important derbies we’ve had in recent years. There’s finals football around the corner, we haven’t been there in a few years so there’s that extra bit of tension that can creep in. The good thing is the boys are level-headed and understand it’s the performance first and the points will be there at the end
