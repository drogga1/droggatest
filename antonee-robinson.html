2018 09 07 Retrieve

[Antonee Robinson sat on a plane heading to the United States three years ago] My dad was the first one who said, ‘you’ll have to learn it. It was one of the first things he said. When I got called up he said, ‘you’ll need to learn the national anthem’.

[The USMNT program came calling in March, around the same time that England’s Under-21 team reached out to him to attend a camp] It just wasn’t even a decision. I was just like, ‘it’s clear to me which country showed more faith in me.’ Not only picking me at the youth level, but picking me for the first team, so I had to show that commitment right back

When I found out I was starting the game it was pretty surreal. I remember at the start of camp I was quite nervous. I wasn’t performing as well as I wanted to be in training. Then the manager called in players into individual meetings and they called me in and they told me, ‘look, we’ve seen you’re quite nervous but you’re starting this game. We want you to be confident. You’re here for a reason so seize it’.

It was like a movie. It was like being stood in the tunnel of a FIFA game and looking at every single star about to come out. It was surreal

[a successful loan stint at Bolton last season was enough to convince the Toffees to sign him to a new deal as a potential future long-term replacement for Leighton Baines] It just showed to me that they give me a lot of faith with a three-year deal. They clearly have it in their mind that I should be starting or at least competing to be their left back. When a team shows you that sort of faith that’s a team you want to stay with

As long as I keep consistently getting better, not only for my club but for the U.S. as well, I’ll be in a good position. If I can keep showing that I can play well and keep developing areas of my game, who knows, then next year that could be the next step for me. Fortunately I’ve gone to a club that plays really good football. They’ve just come up and they’re really ambitious so we’ve gotten off to a great start at the minute

Every time I step onto the field for the U.S. it means something to me. I’m representing what I say is my country now. The fans have been really great towards me. I can understand where they come from in terms of some players wanting to choose which country they want to play for

[And what will he do when the national anthems play that day? Robinson definitely won’t be singing ‘God Save the Queen’] No chance. I’ll watch respectfully, but I won’t sing it

2020 03 12 Retrieve

[USMNT defender Antonee Robinson opens up on how heart problem led to failed Milan move] During my medical for a potential transfer to Milan on deadline day, an irregularity was picked up in my heart rhythm. Further testing was required to ensure the move could be finalised and ratified, though there was not enough time to complete this before the transfer deadline

Following consultation with the club’s medical staff and independent specialists, it has been decided I will need a medical procedure later this month to address the irregularity in my heart rhythm - a procedure called an ablation
