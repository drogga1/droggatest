Desc Striker Guivarc’h is probably the least well known of the 1998 winning side and the one whose name is forgotten in pub quizzes. He made it into the French squad on the back of a superb season for Auxerre, but never really managed to repeat it. He was signed by Newcastle as a World Cup winner in 1998, but played just four times for the Geordie club before being sold to Glasgow Rangers. He returned to Auxerre in 1999 where he scored 25 goals in 60 appearances. He ended his career at Guingamp in 2002 having notched up 14 international caps and just one goal
