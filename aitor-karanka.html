[Aitor Karanka says Haaland is attractive for any club] He is a young striker and he scores goals. It is clear that he has everything and even more at Borussia Dortmund. He is no longer a player who comes from playing in a minor league. I think that any big club is eager to bring in players who score goals. When there is a young player, with goals and at that price, which I think is not very high either, he is attractive for any club
