2019 03 21 Retrieve

[Lucas Paqueta on wearing the 10 shirt of Brazil] Wearing the 10 shirt of Brazil will be a sensation that I will have for the first time. I know my responsibility. I hope to help the team. Ronaldinho, Pele, Zico. So many players have already worn this shirt. Today I have the opportunity and I hope to do my best to meet those expectations. The 10 shirt has a big weight. But the Brazil shirt has its weight. They are five stars. No matter the number

[Paqueta believes the successful switch to Italy is a major factor in his confidence that he can take up the onus of becoming Brazil’s playmaker] This is a very good time for me. Of course I still have to adapt, improve. I’m happy to be part of this evolution of the team. Milan gave me full support and prepared me for this moment. I always want to be playing and I come here 100 per cent to help and give my best. The tactics of Brazil and Milan are similar. In Milan, I play by the left wing, the position I like. Here are great players, and Tite gives me the freedom to play
