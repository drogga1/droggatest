2015 09 12 Retrieve

[Paddy Crerand on his former team-mate and the Golden Globe Award-winning actress] Michael Parkinson was a big United fan. He had a TV programme running in Manchester and he wanted me to go on it. I said as a joke I would only go on if he got Joan Collins. One night Michael phoned me to say he’d booked Joan Collins. I went on the show, she was a lovely lady, but she wanted to meet George Best. So I took George to the television centre, introduced them, and never saw either of them again! The first night George stayed with us, my kids said there was a strange man in the bed. I wonder how many houses that was said in!
