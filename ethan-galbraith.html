2020 04 19 Retrieve

[Man Utd midfielder Ethan Galbraith reveals the first-team player who has helped him the most] Scott McTominay is a similar age to me and will just help me out.

[The Under-23s midfielder made his Northern Ireland debut in September last year against Luxembourg and spoke of his mother’s pride as she cried in the stands at Windsor Park] I’m lost for words. It was absolutely unreal

Obviously I would choose my debut for United, it got me a few more followers on Insta. I thought the experience of my debut would have been worse because of the crowd but as soon as you’re on the pitch you don’t really think about it

[Galbraith will be hoping for more first-team opportunities next season and to follow the path of close friend Mason Greenwood, who has scored 12 goals this term] We’ve been playing together since the Under-16s, so we’re good mates

[However, even if he does make it into the first team on a regular basis he still won’t be selecting himself when playing on FIFA any time soon] I don’t play myself on FIFA. Even next season if I broke into the first team, I still wouldn’t pick myself. The players that would go in my ultimate team would be Pele, Frank Lampard and Ruud Gullit.
