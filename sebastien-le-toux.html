[Sebastien Le Toux on his hilarious imitation of Philadelphia Union team-mate Eric Ayuk’s celebration against New York City FC - after the Cameroonian did Olympic gymnast-esque routine, the quirky Frenchman did the polar opposite] It was a little funny because Eric does so many flips, and I can’t do that. Me and Vincent [Nogueira] were trying to celebrate the goal with him, and I could do nothing more than a nice somersault like this - it’s my only gift as a gymnastic guy! I just wanted to have a laugh, give people a laugh, make a little circus with me and Eric. I’ll work on my somersault more, but it was a pretty good one, I was pretty happy with it
