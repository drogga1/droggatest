[Former World Snooker Champion and Manchester United fan Ken Doherty on Eric Cantona] I walked into the canteen, and he introduced me, and everybody just looked around. The whole room went quiet and I was standing there like a lemon with the [World Snooker Championship trophy]; I didn’t know what to do. There was silence for about 30 seconds, and it felt like an hour. Then this fella gets up off his chair and walks straight over and says [Puts on French accent] ‘Congratulations Mr Doherty’. I was shaking, I was looking up because he was so much taller than me and all I could say was, ‘Thank you Mr. Cantona’. Once he came up everyone followed suit
