[How has a country of just four million people reached the final?] Sport is in our blood. Croats love to compete, to be better than the rest. Also, since we don’t have strong economy, sport is the way to make money, even to find your happiness abroad. That gives young athletes an inner drive [to succeed].

The structure here cannot be compared to somewhere like Germany. But sport in school is very important in Croatia. There are football, basketball and handball pitches at every playground, in every schoolyard

We are a very passionate, emotional people, with very strong patriotic feelings. When you put on a national team jersey, it gives you extra strength. And to see how happy, united and proud people are back home gives you incredible strength and motivation. Playing in a big game for your club doesn’t compare to playing for Croatia

In Croatia, as in Latin America, you still have kids playing in the street, where you want to make fun of your opponent, dribble, create something spectacular. You do not see that in Germany
