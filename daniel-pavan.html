[Liverpool goalkeeper Alisson Becker deserves to win the Ballon d’Or, his former coach Daniel Pavan believes] I believe that because of Alisson’s level of performance, his regularity and winning the Champions League he has total conditions to be a nominee and even deserves to receive the Ballon d’Or. I think it’s a good opportunity to show that goalkeepers also deserve to earn this honour.

[Pavan is also a big fan of Ederson] They are very similar goalkeepers. Both have an excellent game with their feet, know how to get off the goalline very well, are very firm and secure. I think Alisson comes in a better sequence - more top performances for club and country - and is the best goalkeeper in the world today. He has the confidence of [Brazil goalkeeper coach Claudio] Taffarel and [manager] Tite, they rely heavily on him and that’s why he holds the shirt for the Selecao
