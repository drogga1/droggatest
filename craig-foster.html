2017 03 06 Retrieve

[Southern Expansion’s vision and commitment enticed Craig Foster] This is another opportunity now to influence the game very directly. So I believe, very simply, that the professional game in the next 20 years will be the key driver of our success as a sport.

In the second decade, they [MLS] found they needed to make a greater investment. That to get from stage two to three is a significant challenge and that you need tremendous ambition and investment - not just in clubs and players but in infrastructure as well. So that’s at the heart of this bid to take the game forward rather than we’d just like to be a part of the A-League and/or the W-League

We believe in 10 years’ time that [a football-specific stadium] will be a fundamental requirement of any new professional football club, so why not do it now?

2017 03 29 Retrieve

[Former Socceroos midfielder Craig Foster has claimed Southern Expansion will expand the vision of football in Australia] It’s not just about expanding the number of teams but expanding the vision of the game.

So many of the Matildas and the Socceroos, of course, now and historically come from this region. This is one of the greatest talent bases in the country, as yet without their own professional club pathway. That’s what we’re here for. We want to take these kids to the top of the game

We need them to be strong for us to be strong. So we’re saying to our associations ‘we’ll support you at state government, local council and federal government level for your funding support. We want you to have major facilities’. We want the best facilities across this region of any region in the country
