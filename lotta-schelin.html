2020 04 06 Retrieve

[Lyon and Sweden legend Lotta Schelin on Wendie Renard] I came to Lyon in 2008 and Wendie was 18 and even then you could see was going to be a good player. She was so tall, so powerful and she had so much potential, but her heading wasn’t that strong at the beginning. But I saw her working on it, she was so persistent and she said to herself, ‘I can be the best in the world, so I need to work on this.’ A year or two later she wasn’t just a good defender, but she was scoring goals as well. I only won headers once in a while in training against her. If it was a clean duel, it was impossible. I had to use my speed and try to surprise her if I ever played against her at international level, but she reads the game so well, so it was always a challenge.
