[Abhishek Yadav says parallel leagues would benefit the players immensely] In my opinion and this is something which I had mentioned last year as well when the roadmap for Indian football was introduced, that leagues running parallely would have benefit the players. We have to work on a structure where there are several opportunities for players. Only through healthy discussions and collective efforts can we progress as a nation.

I do think that younger players will get more opportunities. In fact, the AIFF and their partners are working on several projects which will create multiple opportunities for a budding player. We will create a strong system so that no talent goes unnoticed
