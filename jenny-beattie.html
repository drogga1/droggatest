Desc In the summer of 2019, the Gunners signed Jen Beattie from Manchester City, who previously played for the Gunners between 2009 and 2013. Beattie slotted straight into the Gunners defence alongside Leah Williamson and the pair formed a complementary partnership. While Leah plays on the front foot and engages attackers, Jen plays the ‘sweeper’ role slightly behind her, mopping up any danger behind Williamson
