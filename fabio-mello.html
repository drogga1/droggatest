[Fabio Mello reveals how Man City failed to sign Fred] The completion of the negotiation of Fred with Manchester United was the culmination of my trajectory, but to arrive at Manchester United I had the need to create a new strategy for the company and that was more or less in 2015/2016

I identified that I needed to create my direct connection with European clubs. At that time, either the market came after our athlete directly or an agent brought the proposal.

That’s when Fred’s project came. He was at a very high level at Shakhtar, called up for the Brazilian national team. There was a refusal to the Manchester City proposal in January 2018 because Shakhtar were in the Champions League quarter-finals. we held the deal, we lined up with Shakhtar and in the window, before the World Cup, we managed to close the deal with Manchester United, who at the time were led by Mourinho. The fact of closing a deal of this magnitude is very gratifying, but the vision to identify and invite Gilberto Silva to start our entry into the European market was crucial. He’s an incredible guy [Gilberto], an idol in England and a reference for Brazilians who play in the Premier League. It was a special moment
