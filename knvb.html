2020 02 08 Retrieve

[Dutch government invests €14m to fight racism in football] Clubs that have made insufficient efforts to combat discrimination may be faced with points deductions, fines or (partly) playing without an audience. Players may be faced with longer suspensions (from matches and training sessions) or exclusion from matches

The KNVB will also set up a special discrimination chamber within the disciplinary committee and the appeals committee. This puts the handling of discrimination cases for both professional and amateur football in the hands of specialists. The special prosecutor assesses incoming reports, conducts preliminary investigations and prepares disciplinary cases on discrimination
