[Dean Henderson admits to not feeling like a Manchester United player, but is happy to be bucking the trend by heading out of Old Trafford in search of game time and recognition elsewhere] Hopefully I can fulfil the ambition of playing for them one day and why not if I keep going the way I’m going?
