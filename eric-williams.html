[He however blamed on the scheduled fixtures] I’d like to say that our team were very tired. [He also expressed his frustration at Pahang’s time-wasting tactics] Well it’s frustrating when you can see the players were putting the pressure on the opponents, and the opponents were obviously wasting time, because it killed our momentum. I am very proud of the players, we had a bad start, they scored two very good goals, our defence was a bit undermanned. But they’re a very good team with a lot of history, whereas it’s our first year [back] in the league
