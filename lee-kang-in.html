Desc South Korean football fans have known for some time that Lee Kang-in is a rare talent, having first seen the winger showcase his skills on a reality TV show at the age of six. Now, though, supporters across the globe are familiar with the Valencia academy product, who claimed the Golden Ball award at last year’s Under-20 World Cup after inspiring his country to a runners-up finish with two goals and four assists. Lee has since gone on to make his senior debut for the Taegeuk Warriors, and become the youngest Korean ever to appear in the Champions League.
