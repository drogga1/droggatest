2019 11 14 Retrieve

[Dinamo Zagreb head coach Nenad Bjelica has revealed that Dani Olmo has already received many offers to leave the club] Dani is impressive both as a footballer and as a person. He’s very modest and ambitious at the same time. He wants to learn every day and I’m proud to have him on the team. I think we are just watching his last matches at Dinamo. There are so many offers. In the end, the club and the player will decide together what is best for both sides. He is ready to play for the best clubs in Europe

2020 01 23 Retrieve

[Dinamo Zagreb manager Nenad Bjelica has confirmed that Dani Olmo is set to join RB Leipzig despite interest from Barcelona] From the numerous offers he had, he picked Leipzig. It was very emotional
