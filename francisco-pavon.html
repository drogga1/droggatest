The one who wins the Ballon d’Or gets it because he deserves it.

[On Marceloa] He arrived with his grandpa and he was such a nice kid. I spent a year-and-a-half with him. [On Roberto Carlos and Marcelo] I played with both. Both are very different even if both are offensive left-backs, but each has his own skills. In their own way, I think they have been the best left-backs ever to play for Real Madrid

Both [Marcelo and Carvajal] are fundamental, especially in attack. Both bring superiority when attacking because they go forward often and create two-versus-one situations. A lot of goals this season have come after long runs from one of them ended with a Cristiano or a [Karim] Benzema shot
