[Alireza Jahanbakhsh speaks about the World Cup’s influence on him and his family’s lives] I have a strong memory of watching the 2006 World Cup at home with my family. I was just a kid at the time; neither of us could have imagined I would do just that only eight years later. But his dream definitely became my dream, and I was really determined to do whatever I could to make it happen
