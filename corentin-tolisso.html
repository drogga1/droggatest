2019 09 09 Retrieve

[Corentin Tolisso says his road back from knee surgery in the last year hardened him and helped him grow mentally] I think it made me grow mentally, it hardened me. I knew that I had to work hard to get back to the top level. I have to continue like this and find my true level before my injury. It depends only on me. I have to give myself a little time. With Bayern, I worked a lot too. I made good preparations. It’s up to me to continue like this. Today, I managed to return to the France team. It’s up to me to prove with Bayern to return again. But it was very emotional. I was very, very proud to be here yesterday

[Tolisso praised Kingsley Coman for his own mental toughness as he has overcame injury to get into fine form] I think it was complicated for Kingsley. I know it’s been difficult, so I’m not going to talk to him about his absence at the World Cup. He is in great shape. What he has been doing since the beginning of the season is exceptional and I hope it will continue like this. We say injuries forge mental toughness and I think Kingsley is very mentally strong
