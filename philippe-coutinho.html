Desc Perhaps the best example of a player helping Liverpool move from the start-of-decade misery to the joy we see now, Coutinho played 152 league games for the club after joining from Inter in January 2013. The tally sees him comfortably edge out current Reds Adam Lallana and Georginio Wijnaldum for the final midfield spot, with both of them down on 120. Coutinho managed to contribute 41 league goals in that time, including a career-high 13 in the 2016-17 campaign as Liverpool finished fourth
