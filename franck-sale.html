[Le Havre chief recruiter Franck Sale believes the Frenchman will definitely seek a new challenge but that all might not be well if he joined Real Madrid] If Paul Pogba has a coach with a strong personality around him, in time there will be a clash somewhere. When he left for Juventus a few years ago, with Alex Ferguson that was very complicated too. Paul didn’t concede because he has such a strong character. So when he says, ‘I’m leaving’, he’s leaving. If tomorrow he goes to a big club like a Real Madrid, he’ll still have his conflicts, even with Zizou [Zidane].

I can see him leaving for another big club searching for this new challenge
