[David Silva has revealed his long-term future is on hold during the coronavirus pandemic, but he will not go back on his decision to leave Manchester City] Everything is on standby, there’s nothing for now. My family ask me all the time but my answer is that everything is stopped. I’m back in training again and looking at places where I feel I can be at 100 per cent. Some friends text me with stories saying I’m going to this place or this other place, but they actually know more than me. Honestly, now I’m waiting that we go through all this situation

My era ends here, but I will always have this club in my heart for everything I’ve lived here. From the first moment I’ve been treated in an incredible way. Those 10 years have even gone very fast now that I look back.

[Man City legend David Silva includes Man Utd rivals in list of greatest team-mates and opponents] The Premier League is a top competition and during these 10 years I played alongside and against very good players.
