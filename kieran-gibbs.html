Kieran Gibbs
Gender Male
Ethnic English
Job English Footballer
Desc Kieran Gibbs is a product of Arsenal’s youth academy - made his senior debut for the club during the 2007/08 season. He became Arsenal’s first choice left-back for the start of the 2011/12 season following the departure of Gael Clichy to Manchester City. But two years later and the 27-year-old, who has 10 England caps to his name, lost his first-team place after the arrival of Nacho Monreal from Malaga and he’s been on the periphery of the first-team ever since
