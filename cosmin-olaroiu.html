2020 02 22 Retrieve

[Real’s late attempt to make Jiangsu pay for Bale after originally agreeing to allow the 30-year-old to join on a free transfer saw the move collapse] It was very close. The club agreed with his agent, his agent was there. We agreed with Madrid and in the last month - I don’t know what happened there, they lose some friendly games by a big score - Madrid changed their mind. First they said they would release him and we would have to pay his salary, then suddenly they said: ‘No, you have to pay for the transfer’. And paying the transfer and his salary was a little bit over the budget and so we looked for another player. But, actually, I know that it was done 90 per cent in the evening, and then in the morning everything changed. But Real Madrid, not us

[Without Bale, Jiangsu finished fourth in the Chinese Super League at the end of last year] We have to deal with this, but we have to also take care of the players.
