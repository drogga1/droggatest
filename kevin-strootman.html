2019 06 02 Retrieve

[Marseille have told Kevin Strootman they will have to sell him in the next transfer window after they failed to qualify for the Champions League] We didn’t get European football, while at Marseille they went out for the Champions League. So the president said in an open and fair conversation: ‘You are an expensive force and without the Champions League it’s difficult for us’. They want to get rid of me, is the message. But I signed a five-year contract last summer and Marseille have paid a big transfer fee to Roma, so it’s not like clubs can take me for a very low price. We have to wait to see what happens in the market and how it will go.

In principle I still have four years of my contact to go. You do not sign somewhere for five years with the intention of leaving again after a year. I want to repay the trust and stay and have a better second year, but then again, you have to get the chance. Marseille also has to deal with UEFA’s Financial fair play rules

[Strootman has already been linked with a return to former side PSV] I have not thought of the Eredivisie yet. My preference is not in a loan move. A sale is preferable
