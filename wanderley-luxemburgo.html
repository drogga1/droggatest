[Brazilian tactician, Vanderlei Luxemburgo, admitted that the only superstar currently playing in the Campeonato Brasileiro is Sao Paulo midfielder Hernanes] For me there is only one superstar in Brazil: Hernanes. He is a player who is good at finishing, and he also helps at the back. I prayed so that Muricy wouldn’t put on another striker. Roque Junior is returning, and we needed his experience. I had to take a risk. I had to take off Mauricio who is 19 years old and I left on Roque, who is 32

2018 10 18 Retrieve

[According to the club’s former coach Vanderlei Luxemburgo, Cristiano Ronaldo’s exit is to blame for Real Madrid’s underwhelming start to the 2018-19 season] It does change. Cristiano makes all the difference. I’ve worked for Real Madrid and I know that such a special player worries opponents - big opponents. Real Madrid don’t have a player now that makes the opponent feel they are dead. You know, that feeling of ‘I have to face Cristiano Ronaldo’. Madrid don’t have such a reference in the current squad

[The pressure is on Lopetegui because of their recent poor run, but Luxemburgo - sacked in December 2005 after 11 months at the helm - thinks Perez will not make a hasty reaction] The pressure is always intense there. We are talking about the biggest club in the world. If the results aren’t coming, the pressure will always be huge, but Florentino Perez is a clever and successful man, and I believe that he knew this would be a difficult season for them. It would never be easy to replicate the previous titles. He knew it. So, I believe that he’s calm now, waiting the right time for doing more investments in the squad to make Madrid champion again
