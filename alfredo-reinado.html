2007 03
After Reinado and his men raided a police post and stole weapons, Mr Gusmao ordered Australian-led troops to track them down. Troops found the group in the town of Same, 50 km (30 miles) south of Dili. Five members of the rebel force were killed in the ensuing clash, but Reinado escaped
2007 08
Reinado met Mr Ramos-Horta, only a few months after he became president, as part of a Swiss-mediated dialogue process. By then Mr Ramos-Horta had ordered troops to stop pursuing Reinado, but he wanted the rebel leader to give up his arms. A task force was formed to co-ordinate the dialogue
2007 11
