Desc Naci Unuvar certainly does not lack belief. Even though Klaas-Jan Huntelaar was on spot-kick duty in Ajax’s KNVB Beker clash with Spakenburg in January, the 16-year-old asked the veteran forward if he could take a late penalty. Unuvar converted with aplomb and, thus, became the youngest goalscorer in the Amsterdam outfit’s history, taking the record away from Clarence Seedorf. Ajax’s only problem now will be holding on to the Netherlands Under-18 international, who has already been scouted by the likes of Barcelona.
