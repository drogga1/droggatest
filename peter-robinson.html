[On Sami Hyypia] It was mid-way through the 1998-99 season when there was a knock on the door of my office at Anfield. I had never met the chap. He came in and introduced himself as a cameraman who covered football in Europe. He knew we were looking for a strong defender and recommended we take a look at Sami, who was playing for Willem, one of the smaller Dutch clubs. That is how it all started. I passed the message on to Gerard Houllier and, over the next few months, members of the staff went to Holland to watch him on several occasions.
