2020 04 16 Retrieve

[Celtic star midfielder Ryan Christie reckons Ireland have pulled off a masterstroke by adding Damien Duff to the backroom staff] Duffers is a quality coach and his training is brilliant. He brings that energy with him every day.

It’s no surprise to me that he has been added to the Republic of Ireland coaching staff. Just being around him and listening to his stories, you realise what a massive career he had at club and international level. We all know he played at an elite level and it’s been invaluable to have someone like him to learn from

I remember watching Duffers play for Ireland. The game that stands out is the World Cup qualifier against France, when Thierry Henry handled the ball before scoring. I can also recall him playing in that successful Chelsea team, even though I was a Manchester United fan. Duffers played alongside some world-class players and his stories are brilliant. When he’s giving advice, everyone listens because he played at the very highest level.

Duffers is also very clear in getting his point across and we all understand what he wants.

[Christie is trying to stay occupied during the football lockdown and admits he finds an Oasis of calm through his acoustic guitar] I actually started learning when I was on loan at Aberdeen a couple of years ago

[Celtic midfielder Ryan Christie believes the club’s pre-season trip to France will provide the perfect preparation for their attempt to make history with a tenth successive Scottish Premiership title] These games are definitely going to be tough. But that’s the level we see ourselves as a club and a team so these games will be great for us. It’s good to test yourself against the best at every chance you get. The friendlies will be the first proper games for us and we’ll take a lot out of those games. We’re not going in against a lower league team or anything like that. [Christie insists motivation, as ever, will not be a problem for the champions] We have a massive motivation within the squad. As long as we take care of that, then everything else will be out of our control.
