2017 05 25 Retrieve

[Former Socceroos defender Mehmet Durakovic - who had a spell in charge of Melbourne Victory from March 2011 to January 2012 - is in charge of Perak in the Malaysia Super League] [Working abroad] just opens up your mind. There’s so much money, there’s so much passion, there’s so much sponsorship. It’s really, really big business here. Football is the number one sport here. You’re dealing with presidents, you’re dealing with big sponsors, you’re dealing with royalty. In Australia, you don’t deal with that.

You’re dealing with people, who are really too busy to worry about football. You have to take charge. So basically, they just give you control of everything, so you have to respect that

One thing about Asia, everybody knows each other and they go on respect, they go on reputation. If you do well, you’ve got a good name. If you don’t, then as a player or a coach, you don’t last long
