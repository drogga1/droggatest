He has scored what you would deem as maybe some fortunate goals were you think he’s just in the right place at the right time. But when that happens over and over, it’s not a coincidence

2019 02 19 Retrieve

[On Concacaf Champions League] I can see why MLS teams have had difficulty in this tournament, and that’s without even having kicked a ball yet. It’s a quick turnaround from MLS Cup. It’s a balance between getting fit versus not wanting to be too sore, too overworked like you usually are in preseason because the games come up very quick. Basically if you get any type of injury in preseason you’re going to miss games. Knowing our fitness needs to be tip top before the season even starts is a challenge as well, but we understand there’s pressure on us as MLS Cup champions to go out there and represent the league well.

When we were watching Champions League in previous years, last year included, you’re rooting for the other MLS teams to do well. You want somebody to win that championship and go represent the league. Hopefully we’ve got the opportunity to make a good run and be that team this year
