Desc The Brazilian burst onto the European scene at the Italian giants following his move from South American football, delivering on his reputation as one of the finest young talents in world football by scoring nine times in his first 18 Serie A appearances. He would be named as the Golden Boy winner the following year after a haul of 15 league goals, and would continue to score regularly in his first four seasons as Milan won the Scudetto in 2011
