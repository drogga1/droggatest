Desc Vujadin Boškov was widely regarded as Sampdoria’s greatest ever coach who held the position between 1986 and 1992. The Yugoslav’s CV boasted spells in charge of his national team as well as assignments in the Netherlands and Spain, including three years at Real Madrid. He was, therefore, no novice, and wasn’t new to Italian football either, having spent a year playing for Samp in the 1960s and two years managing Ascoli prior to taking on the assignment at the Luigi Ferraris
