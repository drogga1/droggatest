February 1998, Roberto Carlos, Real Madrid v Tenerife : A goal that defied the laws of physics. Playing for Real Madrid, the Brazilian with the tree-trunk legs turned the ball into a white projectile on a journey from the dead-ball line just beside the corner flag to the far top corner of Barca’s net

Cup semi-final, 1966, England v Portugal, Bobby Charlton: Sir Bobby scored his second of the match with a blistering first-time shot that cannoned into the top corner. The goal was so good one of his opponents decided to shake hands with him

January 1974, Alan Mullery, Fulham v Leicester: The Fulham midfielder won the BBC’s Goal of the Season competition with this astonishing 25-yard volley from an innocuous-looking cross from the left

November 2001, John Arne Riise, Liverpool v Man Utd: Riise set Anfield’s incendiary atmosphere alight with a smouldering strike to match his flame-red hair. The ball was still rising when it hit the back of the net

August 1967, Pat Jennings, Tottenham v Man Utd: The ultimate long-distance goal. Ball in hand, Jennings punted it downfield, only for it to bounce in front of United goalkeeper Alex Stepney, over his head and into the net
