[Inter chairman Steven Zhang has issued a rallying cry to the club’s support] Christmas is an important time to be together and to be grateful for what we have. We’re not afraid. Today and tomorrow, and with courage, we can compete at the highest levels. Let’s overcome the difficulties and aim for the objectives that we’ve set.
