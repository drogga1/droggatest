[Achim Beierlorzer has been named the new Mainz head coach, just nine days after being sacked by Bundesliga rivals Cologne] It’s funny how football can work out; a week ago I was still at Cologne and now I’m head coach here at Mainz. It’s a team with a very clear football philosophy and it’s one I totally agree with - we want to play a fast game with lots of running and we want to play with a lot of passion.
