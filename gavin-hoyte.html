The first-team players worked with me a lot and talked to me, it was good experience. William Gallas and Kolo Toure told me that the biggest thing for me is talking on the pitch, and being confident on the ball, keep playing your game. So that’s what I need to do. You have to be excited to play against someone like him… He’s a great player and he has played in a lot of big games. He gave us a good test. He is strong so you have to change your game. You can’t get in a physical battle with him because he will just knock you off the ball easily. You have to be intelligent at the back and work your way around that.
