We were under pressure playing at home, as we had to score. Our failure to put away the chances we had resulted in us not being able to play as well as we should have

Thankfully none of my players will be suspended for the second leg despite having received bookings. And those who were injured only picked up knocks, and I expect them to recover in time for the next game
