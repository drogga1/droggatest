[Ander Herrera has hailed the cat-like qualities of Manchester United goalkeeper David de Gea] David has something special. He was born with a gift. There are times in training when I shoot and in my head I’m saying ‘Gol!’ like the Spanish commentators, then this cat-like hand emerges from nowhere and claws it away. He’s made saves in games where I’ve stopped in my tracks and just said to myself: ‘How good is this guy?’ He will make a save and everyone’s singing his name, the TV’s showing six replays, Twitter’s going mad, and he gets up as if it is the easiest thing in the world. In the FA Cup semi-final against Everton in 2016, he saved a penalty from Romelu Lukaku and after the game he’s just like: ‘OK, that happened.
