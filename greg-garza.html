2017 09 10 Retrieve

[Atlanta United concerns about venue switch are surface-level] The people here will make this stadium as crazy as possible. If anyone has ever seen an Atlanta United game at Bobby Dodd, they’ve been waiting to surpass what was expected there. I think they’ll definitely get their money’s worth. The people will make it a wonderful atmosphere and that also helps us on the field. In this league, it’s crazy how different you play at home than you do away. That’s one thing that’s really stuck out in my mind.

It is what it is. I was in Tijuana on an artificial surface for nearly five years. It’s a little different football. We expect it to be a little faster. The ball bounces differently. They’re little details, but we can’t deny they’re there. They’re things that change when you go from a grass surface to an artificial one. But both teams are playing on the same field and we have to start taking advantage of being at home

2018 12 07 Retrieve

[Longtime friends, USMNT hopefuls Greg Garza and Jorge Villafana set to face off in MLS Cup] I don’t call him Jorge, I call him ‘Sueno’, because that’s what I always knew him as from when I was 16 years old and he started coming into national team camps. He wasn’t a left back then, and neither was I. It’s kind of funny to see how we both ended up at the left back position as a pro and, for the both of us, I’m a good friend of his and he’s a good friend of mine.

It’s crazy how much time has passed. It’s been 15 years, and we’re still in touch with most of those guys, and we’re all very proud of that
