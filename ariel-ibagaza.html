Desc Capped twice by Argentina, this attacking midfielder has played in Spanish football since 1998. After five years at Mallorca, he had a three year stint at Atletico Madrid, during which time he won both of his international caps, before returning to the Barralets in 2006. At the beginning of last season he joined Villarreal, where he still plays

2009 04 15 Retrieve

[Ariel Ibagaza says Villarreal Cannot Concede Against Arsenal] It’s vital we keep things tight at the back with no errors in defence. If we concede a goal in London the challenge will become more difficult. Our inspiration is our match in Athens in the previous round. Against a hard rival and the atmosphere we were able to score two goals. This spirit is vital for Wednesday. The team travels in good spirits without fear. We say that Arsenal are a big team but in Villarreal they did not dominate on the pitch. We are not focussing on Cesc Fabregas. It’s true that he’s a key player for the Gunners but we believe they have many dangerous players. We need to shut down a lot of their players and stamp our authority on the match. If we can put in the effort I’m convinced that success is possible. The injuries may have damaged our midfield but now is the real moment for us to demonstrate that our bench has quality. We must now show that we merit a place in the semi-finals

2009 04 21 Retrieve

[Ariel Ibagaza has acknowledged that Villarreal have to win nearly all the games they have left this season if they are to finish in the top four] The table says that we have to win nearly every game, and that is very difficult, because we have to play against strong teams. So, we have to first of all get a win against Recreativo [Huelva] and then see where we go from there. I do not know if the fixture list will be good or bad. We will see when the season is finished, but what is important now is to beat Recreativo and then Getafe. The only thing that is wrong is that we haven’t got the results - simply, we haven’t won. We do seem tired and that makes it worse, and also it seems that everyone else is fresher and better, but we know we have to get results, and we are not doing that

2009 07 28 Retrieve

[Ariel Ibagaza Reveals Ernesto Valverde’s Plan Of Attack For Villarreal] We have been working very well and the pre-season has been very positive so far. Valverde has asked us to press more in attack, move our lines further forward and make it more difficult for our rivals to get out of their own area. It’s very good that he arrived here at the back of a league and cup double with his former team [Olympiakos] and I hope that he can continue that winning streak. When the internationals are back, things will be much easier. They are the most important players for Villarreal and they can help us prepare better for the early rounds of the Europa League

2009 11 12 Retrieve

[Ariel Ibagaza Ready To Return To Action For Villarreal] The truth is that I’m fully recovered. Now I have to use these days to the full so that I can play against Valladolid. I will be playing with a ball in the coming week and I’m very happy to be able to train with the team. I think that if everything goes to plan, I will be available to play in the next match

In the last few matches, the team has risen up a lot. We are on the right track and we are hoping to go on a winning stream to move up to the top. The important thing is to pick up three [points] again and again until the end of the year, and then keep improving in order to finish the league in the top six

2010 05 28 Retrieve

[Villarreal have failed to come to an agreement with Argentinian midfielder Ariel Ibagaza over a new contract and confirmed that he will be seeking a new club this summer] There was no agreement for the contract renewal and Ariel ‘Cano’ Ibagaza will cease to be player of Villarreal on June 30. The negotiations between his agent and the club have not borne fruit and the Argentinian, whose contract terminated this season, will not wear the shirt of the club next season. Villarreal CF wishes to thanks Ibagaza for his services over the past two seasons and wish him luck in his new professional career
