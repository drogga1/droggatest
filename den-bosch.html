2019 11 17 Retrieve

[For their part Den Bosch claimed that the sounds that Moreira heard were not racially motivated, but a crow sound that fans of the team always make at opponents] Let one thing be clear; FC Den Bosch distances itself from everything that has to do with this and acts strongly against stadium visitors who are guilty of this. The so-called ‘crow sounds’ date from the time that Hans Kraay Jr. played for the club. Any opposing player caught offside on the same side of the field as the De Viert stand, is regularly ‘treated’ to such a ‘crow concert’. We are very sorry that Excelsior player Moreira felt personally ill-treated. FC Den Bosch also understands that the referee considered it necessary to protect the player.

Captain Danny Verbeek, teammates and staff were able to take away the feeling that the sounds towards the player were intended to be discriminatory. This allowed the match to be continued
