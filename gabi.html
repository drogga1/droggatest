2018 08 14 Retrieve

[Thibaut Courtois €35 million move from Chelsea to Real Madrid was strange] It hurts. He has played with you, won trophies, it’s a strange one. That’s it, more than it hurts - it’s strange. I prefer to value what we have at home, those who really love this team - Koke, Saul [Niguez], Juanfran, Godin. Godin is indispensable. He cannot leave, and he doesn’t want to. I’ve spoken with him. These offers - United, Juve - exist. I am sure he and Atletico will work it out in the end. Diego is a leader, the ideal captain.

[Gabi also believes that Antoine Griezmann made the right call in sticking with Atletico after a protracted pursuit from Barcelona] I had doubts, it was confusing. He told me: ‘Capi, I’m staying.’ But then I saw [Gerard] Pique, who said: ‘He’s told us he’s coming’. In the end he made the right decision, said no to Barca and stayed. He knows there is nowhere better than Atleti

2018 11 02 Retrieve

[The Ballon d’Or will lose all meaning if Antoine Griezmann is not named the world footballer of the year] If they don’t give (the Ballon d’Or) to him this year, I will not understand anything. For example Cannavaro won Ballon d’Or because he won the World Cup and no one has won so much as Griezmann did this year. He won the World Cup, Supercup, Europa League, been the best in the final, he won a lot. I think If Griezmann doesn’t win, the Ballon d’Or doesn’t make sense.

[Gabi has followed in the footsteps of Raul in moving to play in the nation which will host the World Cup in 2022] This came in a natural way, it was curious. The prince signed Raul and then Xavi. He signed Real Madrid and Barcelona captains and he went crazy when I scored during Lyon final. He said to Xavi that he must sign Atleti’s captain. They called, they were crazy and they made me a huge offer. It was a difficult decision but I decided to start an adventure
