I was going my hardest and he just drove past me. And he was laughing as well, it was demoralising. I had the second quickest time, but he was a different level. I didn’t see him sprint properly. I maybe saw him sprint properly once and that was when he ran the whole length of the pitch in less than 10 seconds.
