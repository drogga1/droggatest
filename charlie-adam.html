[Stoke City midfielder Charlie Adam has claimed Arsenal are too easily outfought and labelled their attacking style of play madness] The thing for Arsenal, I think it’s the front six want to just go and win the game, and they let the four defenders defend, and that’s it, that’s how it becomes.
