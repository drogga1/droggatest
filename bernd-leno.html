2019 11 30 Retrieve

[Real Madrid legend Casillas the ‘first and last’ hero for Arsenal goalkeeper Bernd Leno] My first and last footballing hero is Iker Casillas - he was amazing. I remember the Champions League final in 2002 against my former club Bayer Leverkusen. He played amazingly and was only 18 years old. He was just unbelievable, and he saved Real Madrid the title. Since then he was my idol, and I’ve followed him throughout my career, watching videos. Unfortunately, I’ve never played against him, but hopefully I will, or I will meet him. Maybe he can play again for Porto when he comes back from his injury (Casillas suffered a heart attack in May), but hopefully one day I can meet him. If I only had one question that I could ask him, it would be ‘how have you managed to be so good?’

It’s different and it’s much harder, I think having gained experience in both leagues. In the Bundesliga, like in Europe, the referees protect the goalkeeper. In the Premier League, they don’t. It’s very hard and in the six-yard box there are no fouls - so you have to get used to it. I feel I adapted very quickly to these players who look to block you. I don’t remember this ever happening in Germany, but I quite like it
