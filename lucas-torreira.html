[Arsenal midfielder Lucas Torreira has admitted that it is his ‘dream’ to complete a move to Boca Juniors at some point in the future] I imagine a Copa Libertadores night at the Bombonera wearing Boca’s shirt, of course. Right now my head is in Europe and I want to stay for a while. But I have that desire and dream to go [to Boca] - and not just to retire, but to compete and feel what the Boca world is

My family are big soccer fans and we Uruguayans watch a lot of Argentine football. I had to go to the Madrid final and when the game ended I thought I wasn’t going to be so mad but I was very sad, for the defeat and for how the game had been. It was the time I got to see Boca as a fan. It is true that I have great affection for the club and hopefully it will be forever. In Fray Bentos, I have many friends who are fans of Boca
