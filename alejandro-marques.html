[Barcelona prise Matheus Pereira from Juventus as Alejandro Marques heads in the opposite direction] Born in Sao Paulo and a product of the youth system at Corinthians, Matheus Pereira da Silva is an offensive midfielder who is noted for his touch and his arrival in the area from the second line.

The Brazilian player, who has been followed by Barca club for some time, made the leap to European football in the summer of 2016 at Empoli in Serie B, and was subsequently loaned to Juventus where he would play with Juvenil.

After making a permanent transfer, the Turin club took over his services and sanctioned a number of loan assignments: Girondins de Bordeaux (Ligue 1), Parana Clube and Dijon. Last season, however, Pereira would play with Juventus’ C-Series subsidiary, scoring five goals. In addition, he made his official debut with the first team and took in three appearances. Pereira has been an international with the Brazil U17 team
