2019 03 24 Retrieve

[Manchester City are on the verge of doing something special] We’re on the verge of doing something special. It’s a special squad, that was proved last season. We’ve got a fantastic squad of players. I watch it and stand there in awe of the players. Right the way through the whole squad, they can play, control the ball, pass the ball, it’s as simple as that. It’s a situation where they’re on the verge of being able to complete what they’re wanting to complete. We’ve got a squad of players who can do it but it’s going to be very, very difficult. It’s not easy
