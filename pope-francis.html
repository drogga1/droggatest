2019 04 06 Retrieve

[Pope Francis answers question on television programme La Sexta: Is it a sacrilege to refer to Lionel Messi as ‘God’?] In theory, it’s a sacrilege. You can’t say it and I don’t believe it. I think people say ‘he is God’ just as they say ‘I adore you’. You have to adore only God. It’s expressions that people use. This is a god with the ball on the pitch. It’s a popular way that people have of expressing themselves. Of course, it is a joy [to watch Messi play]. But he is not God
