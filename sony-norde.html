[I-League 2016’s player of the year Sony Norde is all raring to don the famous Green and Maroon of Mohun Bagan for a third successive season] Sure, very happy that I signed for Mohun Bagan again this season. My family supported me and I hope we get success in the league this time.

Sometimes in life, money is important but love is the most important. I have got too much love from Mohun Bagan supporter. Let’s hope for that. That is why Mohun Bagan try to sign the best Indian players to win trophies and us foreign players have to do our best on the pitch and win games

I hope that my experience [in the Copa America] helps my team. We have [Darryl] Duffy, Jeje [Lalpekhlua], Balwant [Singh], Katsumi [Yusa], Sehnaj [Singh] - all good players.
