2017 01 18 Retrieve

[Jason Byrne has his eye on Brendan Bradley’s goalscoring record after signing for Cabinteely ahead of the 2017 season] I would have been retired five years ago only for the record. It’s within touching distance and I suppose it’s my heart ruling my head. I’ll give it another go. If it’s meant to be, it’s meant to be. If it’s not, I can put my hands up and say I tried. I’m not as fit as I once was but the hunger is still there. I don’t want to embarrass myself but ‘Devo’ [Pat Devlin] believes in me and I believe in myself. We’ll give it a good crack.
