What I’ve seen is that we have come a very long way. When I was growing up, there was no such thing as girls’ football. To play, you had to fight to play with boys teams – and that’s what I did. But although the opportunities for girls who want to play the game are so much better, we still have a way to go. And as the national team, we have a duty to lead that effort and to inspire the next generation
