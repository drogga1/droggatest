[Rangers striker Alfredo Morelos has revealed that he intends to head to the Premier League] There are a lot of people watching me from other clubs, but now I have Rangers in my head. Rangers and the national team - I am focused on them. I’m only 22, maybe in five years who knows I might be with a much bigger team, hopefully in England because I am very ambitious. But I am very happy to work with Steven Gerrard. Day-to-day training is excellent. To have such a prominent figure, both for club and country - you have to take advantage of it. I have learned a lot from him and fortunately his feelings towards me have been positive and I am very happy with that. Yes, I am aggressive on the pitch, but it’s manageable. I have to remain calm and there are many positive aspects for me at the moment

[Alfredo Morelos has been linked with the likes of Leicester and Borussia Dortmund] There is a lot of speculation about my possible departure, but there is nothing concrete yet.
