2019 06 05 Retrieve

[Sjaak Swart says Ajax midfielder Donny van de Beek will only leave the club for one of Europe’s top teams] Donny fits a club where attacking footbal is played. Then he is at his best. At Ajax he has that. But eventually every top footballer wants to know where his ceiling his. I don’t see Donny leaving Ajax for just any club abroad. He is not the type who chooses based on money. Donny knows what he has in Amsterdam and he wouldn’t swap that for something less in a sporting sense. If he ever leaves Ajax, in my eyes it must be to go to an absolute top club.
