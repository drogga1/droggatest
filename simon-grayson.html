2020 05 02 Retrieve

[Former Leeds boss Simon Grayson says that the club will be able to attract the likes of Manchester United goalkeeper Sergio Romero should they finally return to the Premier League] If Leeds get back to the Premier League, playing for the club is an exciting prospect for anybody. They’ll be attracting players from all other globe - regardless of whether they’re Argentinian or playing for Manchester United. Getting in front of the Elland Road faithful and working for Bielsa is a massive proposition. It won’t be an issue attracting players. They’ll be having recruitment meetings - Bielsa, Victor Orta and Angus Kinnear. There will be two lists at this moment in time. One for if they make the Premier League and one for the worst-case scenario of staying in the Championship.

[Joe Hart has been linked with a switch to the club] Having that communication with defenders has sometimes been a problem for Leeds. Joe has had a fantastic career. I had him on loan at Blackpool from Manchester City when he was a teenager. He showed what he is capable of back then and went on to have a fantastic career. Whether it’s what Leeds are looking for, I don’t know what Victor has in mind. And knowing them they’ll probably pick someone that nobody has ever heard of.
