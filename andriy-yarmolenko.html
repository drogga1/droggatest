2019 09 10 Retrieve

[West Ham winger Andriy Yarmolenko has admitted that he didn’t like the manner in which Borussia Dortmund parted ways with him after only a year] I’m not the type of guy to be offended, but I didn’t like the way they parted with me. I believe that Dortmund is a great club and should be given great respect by the players on the team. I did not like a lot of things in Dortmund. If you compare it with England, the players have a much better attitude. Any game, even a defeat, was an experience, though. I had both good and bad matches at Dortmund. I believe I got a lot of experience there. I discovered what German football really is. I didn’t expect that I would leave Dortmund so quickly, but now I am very happy that I moved to England. At West Ham, I’m happy
