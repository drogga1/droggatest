[Annie Zaidi tells of the challenges she has overcome to become the only female Muslim coach with a UEFA B coaching license] In a society where everyone is labelled, I’ve got more labels than a jam jar. My next challenge was to get the UEFA B License. It was a life-changing moment and a struggle. When you work towards achieving your dream, it can be a lonely place.
