Desc Slight and unassuming, you’d be forgiven for underestimating Enzo Francescoli. He certainly didn’t look like he cause much of a threat. How wrong you would be. The 80s gave birth to what we now understand to be the modern ‘trequartista’ and Francescoli was one of the best in the business. He was Uruguay’s main man during their back-to-back Copa America wins in 1983 and 1987, before he went on to enjoy a brief, but glittering career in France with Racing Club Paris and Marseille
