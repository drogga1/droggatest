So it’s good to have new faces, you know, freshen things up and bring their quality to the team. From the outside, we’ve kind of been written off in regards to this season and how we’ll go and a lot of other teams have spent a lot of big money and we haven’t.

I think it’s not just the media. As players, there have been campaigns where I’ve been at a team that’s had a very successful pre-season and struggled. Once the games start, that’s when we’ll know and that’s the best way to answer critics is by performing on the Saturday

It was frustrating. We played well enough to win the game [against Norrkoping] but we were just on the wrong end of the [2-1] scoreline, so it’s a disappointing to not have a chance at silverware but it’s funny because we’re still a chance of making Europe if Norrkoping win because they finished in the automatic European spots in the league last year
