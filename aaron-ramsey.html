Desc Aaron Ramsey had the pick of Manchester United and Arsenal in 2008, but chose to join the Gunners after a conversation with Arsene Wenger swung the deal. The Welshman went on to make 369 appearances for the club, scoring 64 goals in the process - including two unforgettable FA Cup final winners in three years against Hull and Chelsea.
