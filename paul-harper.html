[Panini World Cup stickers: How much will it cost to fill Russia 2018 album?] The first sticker you buy is absolutely guaranteed not to be a duplicate. The second sticker you get has a 681/682 [99.85 per cent] chance of being a new sticker. The third sticker you get has a 680/682 [99.7%] chance of being a new sticker, and so on. What is interesting is that to collect just the last 19 stickers for the book, you would still be required to buy 483 packets of stickers, or half the total number of expected packets. Put another way, you are only halfway through when you have just 19 stickers left to collect.
