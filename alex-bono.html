[Alex Bono blossoming in goal for record-setting Toronto FC] My goal this year was to make it very difficult for the coaches to take me out of the lineup when I got the opportunity. That’s how I approach every game, knowing I have to keep performing to keep the job.

[Bono’s rapid ascent began before he was drafted] It was wild and it went by really quickly. I remember I was on my way to the MAC Hermann [Award] ceremony and I get a call from Klinsmann. I thought it was a prank at first. All of a sudden I’m in a locker room sitting across from some of the same veterans who were in the World Cup the summer before

Your first thought is it’s disappointing. You have expectations for yourself, and I believe every player should have high expectations for themselves their rookie year, but then it’s on them to adjust and accommodate accordingly to what the team view is of them. They wanted me to be part of the USL team and get experience and get minutes, and they were valuable minutes. I learned a lot in that first year with TFC II. Just being hard-nosed and being able to deal with the adversity that getting sent down would give somebody

When I finally got the opportunity to come back up, it had a higher value to me to stay there because I knew what it had been like to be sent down and have a bit of a demotion. I definitely had a higher value of my time with the first team, and when I was up there for good, it was important for me to not take it for granted

MLS Cup wasn’t quite a really exciting game in terms of the action part of it, but just being part of an occasion like that is something I’ll never forget. Taking that experience with me and just being part of that team is something that gives you experience even though it wasn’t game experience. I gained the experience of being in that scenario, now this year I know what it’s like to be in a full stadium and what that environment is like, and all the things you have to deal with around the games

Being a part of the national team and playing for the national team is the biggest honor you can have, and just to be a part of that program is an ultimate goal of mine. If you take care of business with your club team, the national team will come along with that
