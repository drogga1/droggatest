[West Ham star Declan Rice has opened up on his shock Chelsea exit at the age of 14] I had to overcome a massive disappointment of being released by Chelsea at the age of 14. It’s been tough because you are used to seeing your teammates, going out every day and doing the day to day things. To have that taken away from you is really tough. But on the other side of it, given the position we are in at West Ham, it’s nice to not feel the pressure of going out and playing for three points every week. It’s nice from that perspective not to have that stress. But really not only me but the whole of the Premier League want to get back to it now to be honest
