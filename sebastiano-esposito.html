Desc It has been a remarkable 12 months for Sebastiano Esposito, who became the youngest player ever to line out for Inter in European competition last March, at 16 years and 255 days, before then firing Italy to the final of the European Under-17 Championship in May. The Nerazzurri rewarded the academy product with a three-year contract during the summer, and he netted the first of what should be numerous goals for the club in a 4-0 win over Genoa in December.
