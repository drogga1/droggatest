I think the ‘quinto partido’ is much more something the fans and the press [chat about]. The press is a big part of why we talk about this fifth game. Obviously the players do have it in mind. In 1994 we were right there and we’ve been very close to advancing there in other World Cups

I think as a player, you concentrate more on the games that are coming, on moving on, but obviously you have the expectation of getting to this ‘quinto partido’ - not only the ‘quinto partido’​ but advancing ideally to the seventh game, right?
