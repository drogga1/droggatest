[Sepp De Roover on Belgian League] A lot of players leave Belgium for bigger leagues, like [Axel] Witsel and [Marouane] Fellaini. The Pro League has become a feeder league over the years. Belgian clubs have yet to find an answer to the problem of their best players leaving

The bigger clubs from abroad are willing to take a gamble with signing young players. They often sign a lot of youngsters between the age of 16 and 20 and there are always some who will come good, like you see with [Adnan] Januzaj. He came through the ranks at Anderlecht, but is now a starter at Manchester United at the age of 18. It’s impossible to compete with clubs like this

I think it will be very hard for Belgian clubs to become competitive again in Europe, also because of the financial gap with the bigger leagues. Clubs like Manchester United and Barcelona have huge budgets and Belgian sides can’t compete with that
