[Phoenix from the flames - Leon Bailey back to his best after injuries and suspension] Last season I had ups and downs. I had an injury towards the end, so it wasn’t my best season, but right now I’m really looking forward to the new season

If you’ve been injured for so long and then see the red card in the first game back, it’s not a good feeling. There are moments when you doubt yourself. But I have people around me who support me 100 per cent. The coach always motivates me a lot, I motivate myself. And that helped me a lot to come back and help the team

[After the disappointment of last season, Bailey is aiming higher this season] I see our team being at the highest level. We have, of all the teams right now, the best chance to be in the top three this year. The Bundesliga level is high right now and the points are very close, and even the teams that are behind are doing their best to beat teams ahead of them which makes it more complicated but it’s a very intense league and I’m happy to be a part of it.
