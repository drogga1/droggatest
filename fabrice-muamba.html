2020 04 23 Retrieve

[Fabrice Muamba feared the worst when he saw Swansea City striker Bafetimbi Gomis collapse at White Hart Lane] I am sure he will not be allowed to play for a couple of weeks or even a couple of months. I am 100 per cent sure he’ll see a specialist. He will probably see three specialists and two of them need to give him the all-clear before he can play again. It’s very different to what happened to me. Bafetimbi Gomis has a history of medical issues. I did not have a history, so it was a real surprise when it happened to me.
