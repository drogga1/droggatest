Desc Seung-woo Lee was South Korea’s huge hope for the future in 2011 when he traded Incheon United for La Masia. He played just one game for Barcelona’s B side before being sold to Hellas Verona for just €1.5m (£1.3m/$1.7m) in 2017. His international career has been more impressive than his club form, helping South Korea win the Asian Games in 2018 by assisting Heung-min Son’s winning goal in the final
