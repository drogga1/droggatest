[Marco Verratti insists that his Paris Saint-Germain team-mates are not interested in financial gain, while expressing his dream of winning the Champions League with the French side] At the start and even now, there are a lot of players who tell me, ‘come on, talk to the president, I want to come and play there’. Some say we play at PSG for the money but it’s not the case. If a player plays for money, he’ll go to China. It is quite difficult at PSG because when you lose a game there is a lot of pessimism. It is true that in the league we are used to winning, we have won a lot for the last seven years.
